The Avatar is the human embodiment of light and peace through the connection
with the Avatar Spirit. It is the central identity in the Avatar Cycle: when an
Avatar dies, the Avatar Spirit causes reincarnation into the next nation in the
cycle of fire, air, water, and earth.

The page also establishes the Avatar State as the high-power mode that draws on
the skills and knowledge of all past lives, and notes that the connection to
past lives can be severed. Wan and Raava are the origin point of the cycle.

## Vault Use

This is the series anchor for the Avatar rulesystem. The direct cycle relatives
that matter for the vault are Wan, Raava, Past Lives, and the Avatar State.
