The Ifrit are the evil-djinn tribe most clearly associated with bad luck and malice.

The page describes them as the most malicious of the djinn tribes and ties them to the use of gambling and casinos as instruments of misfortune. Their leadership changes across the series, but the tribe remains the clearest evil counterpart to the good tribes.
