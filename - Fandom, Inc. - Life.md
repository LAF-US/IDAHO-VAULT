**Life**, when written with a capital "L," is used to refer to the realm of the "real world," differentiating it from the realm of [Death](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death"). Beings can travel from Death into Life, and vice versa. It is also the name the of the force that the [Dead](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Dead "Dead") need to feast upon, usually by killing people violently, to stave off a constant pull back to Death.

Occasionally, "Life" is also used to refer to all living beings, with the capital "L" as a sort of honorific. (For example, during *[Abhorsen](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Abhorsen_\(Book\) "Abhorsen (Book)")*, the [Disreputable Dog](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/The_Disreputable_Dog "The Disreputable Dog") states that should [Orannis](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Orannis "Orannis") be unbound, it would mean "the end of all Life.")
