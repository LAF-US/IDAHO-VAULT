Zuko is the Fire Nation prince whose captivity and escape are part of the
episode's parallel plot. The episode uses him as a counterpoint to the Avatar
State crisis, and the same canon page later shows his long-term role as Aang's
ally and firebending mentor.

## Vault Use

This is the parallel-rules surface for the episode's political pressure plot.
