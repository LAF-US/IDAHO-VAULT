*Children of the Lamp* is the Fandom-facing series page for P. B. Kerr's djinn novels.

The page frames the series around John and Philippa Gaunt, twins who discover they are djinn and are drawn into a larger world of inherited power, training, danger, and family obligation. It is the series-level surface: the overview that orients the reader before the more specific metaphysics pages.

## Vault Use

This source gives the series header and the broad narrative frame. The djinn and lamp mechanics belong in their own page-level source notes.
