A djinn seal is a binding form, usually made of jade or copper.

The page distinguishes ordinary seals from living seals, with the living seal presented as especially dangerous because breaking or melting it kills the creature and releases the word used in the binding. That makes the seal both object and control surface.

For the vault, this is a clean metaphor for durable authority tokens: a seal is a bound object whose material and rule behavior matter.
