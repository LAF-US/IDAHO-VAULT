The **Poe family** is a family that consists of five characters within [the series](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/A_Series_of_Unfortunate_Events "A Series of Unfortunate Events"). [Arthur](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Arthur_Poe "Arthur Poe") and [Polly](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Polly_Poe "Polly Poe") (renamed to [Eleanora](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Eleanora_Poe "Eleanora Poe") in the series), the parents, hold powerful positions in [the City](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_City "The City") though both of them seem easily influenced. They have two kids, named [Edgar](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Edgar_Poe "Edgar Poe") and [Albert](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Albe***REMOVED***Poe "Albert Poe").

## Members

- [Arthur Poe](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Arthur_Poe "Arthur Poe")
- [Eleanora Poe](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Eleanora_Poe "Eleanora Poe") (Poe's sister in the books, and his wife in the TV series)
- [Polly Poe](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Polly_Poe "Polly Poe") (Poe's wife in the books)
- [Edgar Poe](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Edgar_Poe "Edgar Poe")
- [Albert Poe](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Albe***REMOVED***Poe "Albert Poe")

The family seems to have been friendly with [Beatrice](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Beatrice_Baudelaire "Beatrice Baudelaire") and [Bertrand Baudelaire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Bertrand_Baudelaire "Bertrand Baudelaire"), as their children remember them being invited for dinner often, and Arthur was put in charge of the Baudelaires' estate until [Violet Baudelaire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Violet_Baudelaire "Violet Baudelaire") could come of age.

While not much is known about the family of Arthur Poe in the books, it is mentioned that Edgar and Albert are very rude,[^1] and Polly seems to be fond of In things.[^2] Poe's sister, Eleanora, is the head editor of *[The Daily Punctilio](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Daily_Punctilio "The Daily Punctilio")*, though Poe sometimes disagrees with her about her handling of the Baudelaire case.[^3]

In the Netflix series, the characters of Polly and Eleanora were combined, likely for simplification and time constraints. (Their role was also merged with [Geraldine Julienne](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Geraldine_Julienne "Geraldine Julienne").) Thus, the Poe family has stronger connections to *[The Daily Punctilio](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Daily_Punctilio "The Daily Punctilio"),* and Poe seems less skeptical of his wife's competence with her job as a journalist.

In the Netflix canon, it is also mentioned of Edgar and Albert that one of the brothers followed their father into the banking world, while the other "lives in a cave and talks to sheep," and each thinks the other has it better.[^4]

## Trivia

- Their surname is likely a reference to [Edgar Allan Poe](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Edgar_Allan_Poe "Edgar Allan Poe"), a famous poet, as are Edgar and Albert's first names.
- Edgar, Albert and Eleanora make no appearance in the [2004 film](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Lemony_Snicket%27s_A_Series_of_Unfortunate_Events_\(film\) "Lemony Snicket's A Series of Unfortunate Events (film)"), though Polly makes a small cameo.
