The **Caliban family** consist in the couple [Thursday](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Thursday "Thursday") and [Miranda Caliban](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Miranda_Caliban "Miranda Caliban"), and their daughter, [Friday Caliban](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Friday "Friday"). [Olivia Caliban](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Olivia_Caliban "Olivia Caliban") is also part of the family, though her exact relation is unknown.[^1]

## Members

- [Thursday Caliban](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Thursday "Thursday")
- [Miranda Caliban](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Miranda_Caliban "Miranda Caliban") (†?)
- [Olivia Caliban](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Olivia_Caliban "Olivia Caliban") †
- [Friday Caliban](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Friday_Caliban "Friday Caliban") (†?)
- [N](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/N._Snicket "N. Snicket") (possible)
- [Vice Principal Nero](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Vice_Principal_Nero "Vice Principal Nero") (possible)
- [Monday](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Monday "Monday") (possible)

It is possible that the people in Thursday's family are named after days of the week, following each other; Friday, his daughter, is named for the weekday after his. This could mean that [Count Olaf](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Olaf "Count Olaf") 's associate, Monday, is a member of the family.

### Theories

- **[Snicket Clan](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Snicket_Clan "Snicket Clan")**
	- It is theorized that Miranda and Olivia are sisters, and the M and the O on the [Snicket Clan](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Snicket_Clan "Snicket Clan") tree from *[Lemony Snicket: The Unauthorized Autobiography](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Lemony_Snicket:_The_Unauthorized_Autobiography "Lemony Snicket: The Unauthorized Autobiography")*. This would make them the daughters of [F. Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/F._Snicket "F. Snicket"), the granddaughters of [Chas. Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Chas._Snicket "Chas. Snicket"), and the sisters of [N](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/N._Snicket "N. Snicket").[^2]
		- This theory could be possible in the book canon; it is probably untrue in the Netflix canon, as this would make them first cousins of the Snicket siblings, and in the Netflix series, [Jacques Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Jacques_Snicket "Jacques Snicket") and Olivia Caliban have a romantic relationship.[^3]
		- N's supposed identities are unknown, except that they are speculated to be [Vice Principal Nero](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Vice_Principal_Nero "Vice Principal Nero"), who does not seem to be a V.F.D. member and does not run a school which seems to be a V.F.D. location. However, it is possible that he was their brother, due to the fact that all of their names coincidentally originate from Italy.
		- Something that deviates from this theory is that Nero was unaware that Mrs. K was [Kit Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Kit_Snicket "Kit Snicket") (when she abducted both [Duncan](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Duncan "Duncan") and [Isadora Quagmire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Isadora_Quagmire "Isadora Quagmire")), who is supposed to be N.'s first cousin.
		- Nero's last name was known as "Feint", which most likely makes him part of the Feint Family, rather than the Calibans.
				- There is a theory that F was a member of the Feints, with the surname Feint starting with the same initial in F's name, although this is highly unlikely.
- **[Widdershins Family](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Widdershins_Family "Widdershins Family")**
	- Miranda Caliban is [Mrs. Widdershins](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Mrs._Widdershins "Mrs. Widdershins"), and she retreated to the island, leaving Widdershins to claim she was dead. This would make [Fernald](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Fernald "Fernald") and [Fiona](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Fiona "Fiona") siblings or half-siblings of [Friday Caliban](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Friday_Caliban "Friday Caliban"), depending on the identity of [Thursday](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Thursday "Thursday"); many theorists believe that Thursday may be Widdershins himself, as Widdershins's first name and Thursday's surname are not revealed.
		- Both Miranda and Widdershins use the excuse of a "manatee accident" to explain the disappearance of their partner.[^4]
		- Fernald, Fiona and Friday have alliterative names, presenting a pattern. Another fact supporting this theory is that most siblings in the series come in groups of three, and even on the rare occasions that only two siblings are known of, the existence of a third sibling is usually hinted at (such as the [White-Faced Women](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/White-Faced_Women "White-Faced Women")). The only known siblings in groups of two (aside from Fiona and Fernald) are from *[All the Wrong Questions](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/All_the_Wrong_Questions "All the Wrong Questions")*: [Pip](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Pip_Bellerophon "Pip Bellerophon") and [Squeak Bellerophon](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Squeak_Bellerophon "Squeak Bellerophon"), [Kellar](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Kellar_Haines "Kellar Haines") and [Lizzie Haines](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Lizzie_Haines "Lizzie Haines"), [Zada and Zora](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Zada_and_Zora "Zada and Zora"), and [Tatiana](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Tatiana_Cozy "Tatiana Cozy") and [Treacle Cozy](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Treacle_Cozy "Treacle Cozy").
		- Evidence against this theory could be that [Kit Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Kit_Snicket "Kit Snicket") believes Friday's father and Miranda's husband, Thursday, is alive and well, and mentions dining with him the day before meeting the Baudelaires. The day before meeting the Baudelaires, Captain Widdershins was with them on his submarine, and by the time Kit was discussing Thursday, Kit believed the Captain to be either deceased or in mortal peril due to the crash of the *[Queequeg](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Queequeg "Queequeg")* and arrival of the [Great Unknown](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Bombinating_Beast "Bombinating Beast").[^1]

## Trivia

- The name *Caliban* comes from [a character of the same name](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caliban "wikipedia:Caliban") in [William Shakespeare](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Shakespeare "wikipedia:William Shakespeare") 's *[The Tempest](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Tempest "wikipedia:The Tempest").*
	- The play concerns a group of people stranded on a deserted island, much like Miranda, Friday and the other colonists of [The Island](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Island "The Island").
		- In *The Tempest,* [Caliban](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caliban "wikipedia:Caliban") is a monstrous character who serves as the primary antagonist; Shakespeare seems to have created the name specifically for this character, and comes from the Spanish word *canibal*.
		- Miranda's name is also taken from this play: In *The Tempest*, [Miranda](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Miranda_\(The_Tempest\) "wikipedia:Miranda (The Tempest)") is a girl who was raised on the island by her father, ignorant of the fact her father is an exiled duke and she should rightfully be part of the royal family of Milan. This could be considered a parallel to Friday's ignorance of the survival of her father and her mother's past on the mainland.
		- Several other islanders are also named after characters from the play- [Alonso](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Alonso "Alonso"), [Ariel](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Ariel "Ariel") and [Ferdinand](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Ferdinand "Ferdinand"). Alonso also mentions a man in his past named [Gonzalo](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Gonzalo "Gonzalo"), and Olaf uses the name [Stephano](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Stephano "Stephano") for a disguise.
		- Interestingly, Olivia's first name is also shared with [a prominent Shakespearean character](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Olivia_\(Twelfth_Night\) "wikipedia:Olivia (Twelfth Night)"), though this time from *[Twelfth Night](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Twelfth_Night "wikipedia:Twelfth Night")* instead of *The Tempest.*
- It is currently unknown whether the surname Caliban comes from Thursday or Miranda's family.
- It is possible that Thursday's father or mother is named Wednesday.
