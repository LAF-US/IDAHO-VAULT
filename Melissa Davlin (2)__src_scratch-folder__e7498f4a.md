[[Melissa Davlin]] is the lead producer of [[Idaho Reports]] and president of the [[Idaho Press Club]] who co-signed the [[Capitol Correspondents Association CCA Letter March 2026|March 2026 CCA letter]] to [[Joint Finance-Appropriations Committee|JFAC]] co-chairs.

## Basic Info

- **Role:** Lead producer, [[Idaho Reports]] / [[Idaho Public Television|IPTV]]; Idaho Press Club president
- **CCA letter:** Co-signed as IPC president (not as CCA officer) alongside [[Kevin Richert]]

## Editorial Constraint

As [[Idaho Reports]] lead producer, Davlin cannot editorially own the JFAC working groups story due to IPTV's status as a state agency under [[State Board of Education|SBOE]] oversight. Primary story carrier: [[Clark Corbin]] ([[Idaho Capital Sun]]). Logan Finney coordinated the CCA research and briefing document.
