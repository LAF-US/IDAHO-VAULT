Simpkin, a known Foliot

**Foliots** are with some intelligence and power but are considerably less than those of [Djinn](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Djinn "Djinn"), and are more potent than [Imps](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Imps "Imps"). [Bartimaeus](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Bartimaeus "Bartimaeus"), an egomaniac, view them with some disdain. And he described them as "cut-price Djinnis" since they have the same yet toned-down skills. They are, at least, easier to control than djinn for a magician, and are sometimes favoured by magicians for their stealth and cunning.

Foliots are somewhat prone to petty squabble but are apparently capable labourers and due to their being reasonably adept at changing shape, they make excellent spies.
