An **Isthmus Anchor** is an object used to open a portal to another dimension. It must be from one reality, but reside in another. For example, Skulduggery's skull was used to open a portal to the dimension he was in himself. To open up a portal, however, you would also need a [Teleporter](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Teleporter "Teleporter").

## The Grotesquery

![](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/477px-Grotesquery.jpg)

The Grotesquery

*Main article: [The Grotesquery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Grotesquery "The Grotesquery")*

The Grotesquery is used as an Isthmus Anchor in *[The Faceless Ones](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Faceless_Ones "The Faceless Ones")*. [The Diablerie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Diablerie "The Diablerie") kidnapped [Fletcher](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Fletcher_Renn "Fletcher Renn") and forced him to open up the portal for the [Faceless Ones](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Faceless_Ones "Faceless Ones") to return. After all the gods were defeated, [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") threw it back into the portal but was then taken in the portal himself. The Grotesquery was no longer an Isthmus Anchor.

*Main article: [Skulduggery Pleasant's skull](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant%27s_skull "Skulduggery Pleasant's skull")*

Skulduggery's skull is the second Isthmus Anchor and was used to open up the same portal in *[Dark Days](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_Days "Dark Days")*. [Wreath](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Solomon_Wreath "Solomon Wreath") suggested to use the skull in the third book. Valkyrie, with the help of Fletcher and [China](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/China_Sorrows "China Sorrows"), used the skull to get Skulduggery back from the Faceless Ones. Afterwards, Skulduggery was reunited with his old skull.
