# Tree Hollow Leaflet

This note preserves the small leaflet left in the hollow of a tree after the
spring and before the fork in the road.

## Leaflet Text

Road continues under canopy.

Spring on the right-hand side, clear and drinkable.

Bench sound. Quiet here is companionable, not empty.

Keep to the cobbles after dark.

`WHO` bites when unanswered.

`WHERE` is a crossing, not a promise.

Witness before declaration.

If the path still holds beneath your feet, continue.

## Field Note

This is not doctrine.
It is trail-literature: practical witness left for the next traveler before the
forest deepened and the road divided.
