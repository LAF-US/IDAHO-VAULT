The Jann are a good-djinn tribe aligned with good luck and opposed to chaos.

The page is brief, but it is useful as a tribe-level confirmation that the series organizes djinn into multiple aligned groups. The Jann surface sits alongside Marid and Jinn as part of the good-side order.
