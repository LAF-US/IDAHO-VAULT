Past lives are the prior Avatars in the Avatar Cycle. They can appear to the
current Avatar or others, and they can impart knowledge, warnings, and
memory-based continuity. Their connection can be weakened or severed.

The past-life chain is not just ancestry. It is the inherited memory surface of
the Avatar identity.

## Vault Use

Past Lives are the continuity archive that the current Avatar can query when
the bridge is intact.
