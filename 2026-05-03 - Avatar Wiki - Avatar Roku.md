Avatar Roku is the immediate predecessor of Aang. In the Avatar State material,
Roku is the Avatar who appears to Aang to explain the danger and function of
the Avatar State: it is a defense mechanism that draws on the skills and
knowledge of past lives, but death in that state breaks the reincarnation
cycle.

## Vault Use

This is the direct mechanical advisor surface for the Avatar State episode.
