War bond text:

Kootenai Nation - Declaration of War - Know all ye that on this 20 September
1974 that a state of war does now exist between the Kootenai Nation and the
United States of America - signed Amelia Cutsack Trice, tribal chairwoman, and
Douglas James Wheaton Sr., tribal representative - "Justice-Balance-Liberty"
