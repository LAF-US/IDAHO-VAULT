**Sanctuaries** are the organizations that enforce the [magical](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") laws. All Sanctuaries have a [Council of Elders](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Council_of_Elders "The Council of Elders") that lead the Sanctuary, and the Council is in turn lead by a [Grand Mage](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Grand_Mage "Grand Mage"). Sanctuaries were seen by some as a "magical police".

## History

Before the Sanctuaries existed, there were magical communities. Each of these was ruled by twelve village elders. Each of these twelve would oversee a different aspect of village life, but, when the time came to make important decisions, all twelve votes were counted equally. When the Sanctuaries were established, the unwieldy twelve were cut down to a small council of three.

At first the Sanctuaries were unofficial. The sorcerers would go to the Sanctuaries to escape the [Mortals](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mortals "Mortals") ' world. Over time, the sorcerers came up with rules, leaders and a code of conduct.[^1]

Although the Sanctuaries have worked together before, there has been a lot of rivalry, particularly against [Ireland](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Ireland "Ireland"), as it is seen as a Cradle of Magic. Although the Sanctuaries (such as [the American Sanctuary](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_American_Sanctuary "The American Sanctuary")) claimed it was due to the danger posed by a Cradle of Magic in distress, some speculated it was due to their power-hungry nature that the wanted to take over Ireland.

In *[Dark Days](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant:_Dark_Days "Skulduggery Pleasant: Dark Days")*, the Irish Sanctuary was destroyed by the [Desolation Engine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Desolation_Engine "Desolation Engine") (which was restored by a Remnant-possessed [Kenspeckle Grouse](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Kenspeckle_Grouse "Kenspeckle Grouse")). In *[Mortal Coil](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mortal_Coil "Mortal Coil")*, following a Council re-election, the Sanctuary was relocated to [Roarhaven](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Roarhaven "Roarhaven"). This was later revealed to be part of [the Man with Golden Eyes](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Man_with_Golden_Eyes "The Man with Golden Eyes") 's master plan.

## Sanctuary Relationships

- While [Thurid Guild](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Thurid_Guild "Thurid Guild") was the Grand Mage of the [Irish Sanctuary](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Irish_Sanctuary "Irish Sanctuary"), he has had many meetings with the Russian Sanctuary.
- In *[Mortal Coil](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mortal_Coil "Mortal Coil")*, Ireland was tipped of by a source in the German Sanctuary that the American Sanctuary were planning to take over Ireland.
- The American Sanctuary has on many occasions threatened to take over Ireland if bad things continue continue to happen there.
- [Davina Marr](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Davina_Marr "Davina Marr") transferred from [Prime Detective](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Prime_Detective "Prime Detective") in the American Sanctuary and joined the Irish Sanctuary.
- In *[Last Stand Of Dead Men](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Last_Stand_Of_Dead_Men "Last Stand Of Dead Men")*, almost every Sanctuary in the world formed a [Supreme Council](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Supreme_Council "Supreme Council") by grouping together and [invaded Ireland](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_War_between_Sanctuaries "The War between Sanctuaries").
