The **Southerlings** are a displaced people who attempt to settle in [Ancelstierre](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Ancelstierre "Ancelstierre") and then the [Old Kingdom](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Kingdom "Old Kingdom") when their homeland is devastated by war. However, this war is a result of [Hedge](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Hedge "Hedge") 's plan to free [Orannis](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Orannis "Orannis"), and many of the Southerlings end up being killed and used as [Dead Hands](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Dead_Hands "Dead Hands") to excavate the Lightning Trap.

In *[Lirael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Lirael "Lirael")*, Sabriel and Touchstone fear that Hedge will attack the Kingdom with all the Southerling refugees (around 200,000). Instead, in *[Abhorsen](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Abhorsen "Abhorsen")*, Hedge uses his massive host of Dead Hands to make two rows through the [Wall](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Wall "Wall"), in order to carry Orannis's hemispheres and a possessed [Nicholas Sayre](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Nicholas_Sayre "Nicholas Sayre") between them. The Wall's protective [Charter magic](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Charter_magic "Charter magic") is occupied with destroying the Hands (which are constantly replaced) so that Nick and Orannis can pass through unharmed, when otherwise such a powerful [Free Magic](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Free_Magic "Free Magic") being would be unable to cross.

During *[Goldenhand](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Goldenhand "Goldenhand")*, it is noted that [Prince Sameth](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Sameth "Sameth") is attempting to find lands for the Southerlings. However, it is noted that because the Southerlings don't believe in magic, the process of rehousing is extremely difficult.
