**Essence** is the substance that makes up a [demon](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Demons "Demons"). Whilst they are in the human world, their essence is continually dissipating. Touching silver or iron also burns a spirit's essence. Upon returning to [the Other Place](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/The_Other_Place "The Other Place"), spirits' essences gradually heal from their stay on Earth.

## Trivia

- Beings made of [energy](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Spirits "Spirits") can shape-shift because they are a services instead of a goods (which have mass).
