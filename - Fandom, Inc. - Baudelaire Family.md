**The Baudelaire Family** is a family that is central in *[A Series of Unfortunate Events](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/A_Series_of_Unfortunate_Events "A Series of Unfortunate Events")* and associated with [VFD](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Volunteer_Fire_Department "Volunteer Fire Department").

## Members

- [Beatrice Baudelaire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Beatrice_Baudelaire "Beatrice Baudelaire") †
- [Bertrand Baudelaire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Bertrand_Baudelaire "Bertrand Baudelaire") †
- [Violet Baudelaire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Violet_Baudelaire "Violet Baudelaire")
- [Klaus Baudelaire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Klaus_Baudelaire "Klaus Baudelaire")

- [Bertrand Baudelaire's Male Cousin](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Bertrand_Baudelaire%27s_Cousin "Bertrand Baudelaire's Cousin")
- Bertrand Baudelaire's Female Cousin
- [Mr. Fagin](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Fagin "Mr. Fagin")

The Baudelaire family is widely known to have fast-acting peppermint allergies.[^3] The fact that Beatrice had these allergies,[^4] as well as the fact all of [Lemony Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Lemony_Snicket "Lemony Snicket") 's letters to Beatrice are addressed "BB", presumably means that Beatrice was born into the Baudelaire family, and Bertrand took her surname when they married.

It is a family tradition to name their children after the deceased.[^5]

Both Beatrice and Bertrand were known to be Volunteers of [VFD](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Volunteer_Fire_Department "Volunteer Fire Department"); however, their children were isolated from knowledge of the organization, and only Violet has the barest memories of associated phrases.[^6] The Baudelaires were also angry and scared when Klaus reached some of their secret books, presumably ones linking to the organization.[^7] This implies that Beatrice and Bertrand left and did not want their children to get involved in the organization, presumably in the hope that they could live normal lives. It is unknown whether or not the Baudelaire orphans still consider themselves volunteers following the events of *[The Penultimate Peril](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Penultimate_Peril "The Penultimate Peril")*, though it seems that Beatrice Baudelaire II may have managed to take VFD classes when she got older.[^4]

Beatrice was engaged to [Lemony Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Lemony_Snicket "Lemony Snicket"), but broke up with him for unknown reasons and married Bertrand instead.

Bertrand is known to have been adopted.[^5] While unknown whether through his adoptive or biological family, his [cousin](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Bertrand_Baudelaire%27s_Cousin "Bertrand Baudelaire's Cousin") married [the sister](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Monty_Montgomery%27s_Sister "Monty Montgomery's Sister") of [Monty Montgomery](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Monty_Montgomery "Monty Montgomery").[^2]

[Mr. Fagin](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Fagin "Mr. Fagin"), one of the people who Mr. Poe considers to be the orphans' guardian after The Ersatz Elevator, is the orphans' nineteenth cousin. This would make him Beatrice and Bertrand's nineteenth cousin once removed.

[Count Olaf](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Olaf "Count Olaf") claims to be either a fourth cousin three times removed or a third cousin four times removed of the Baudelaire children; it is unknown which parent, if either at all, he was related to.[^8]

The Baudelaire children’s second cousins were [Gregor](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Gregor_Anwhistle "Gregor Anwhistle") and [Ike Anwhistle](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Ike_Anwhistle "Ike Anwhistle"); Ike was married to [Josephine Anwhistle](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Josephine_Anwhistle "Josephine Anwhistle").[^3]

Violet, Klaus and Sunny Baudelaire adopted the daughter of [Kit Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Kit_Snicket "Kit Snicket"), whom they named after their mother.

### Speculation

Both Bertrand's biological and adoptive families are currently unknown. A common fan theory is that he was adopted by [S. Theodora Markson](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/S._Theodora_Markson "S. Theodora Markson"), his chaperone,[^9] though this is not confirmed.

It is speculated that Gregor and Ike Anwhistle are the G and the I on the [Snicket Clan](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Snicket_Clan "Snicket Clan") family tree, meaning that their mother, who is known to have one eyebrow and one ear,[^3] is probably [D. Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/D._Snicket "D. Snicket"), which would distantly connect Beatrice to the Snickets.[^10] How distantly the connection is depends on if the Anwhistles' father or mother is her cousin.

## Trivia

- The surname "Baudelaire" is taken from [Charles Baudelaire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_Baudelaire "wikipedia:Charles Baudelaire"), a French poet and author who composed notable works such as *[Les Fleurs du mal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Les_Fleurs_du_mal "wikipedia:Les Fleurs du mal").*
	- The word "baudelaire", itself, is of French origin, and is the name of a short cutting sword or dagger with a broad, slightly curving blade.[^11]
- [Daniel Handler](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Daniel_Handler "Daniel Handler") has confirmed in an interview that the Baudelaire Family are Jewish, but whether they are ethnically and/or religiously Jewish is not stated.[^12]
