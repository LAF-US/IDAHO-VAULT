Josh Tanner is the Republican Idaho House member from District 22A (Eagle) serving as JFAC House co-chair and co-recipient of the CCA open-meetings letter in March 2026.

## Basic Info

- **District:** 22A (Eagle/Meridian, western Ada County)
- **Chamber:** House
- **Role:** JFAC House co-chair (2025–present); preceded by Wendy Horman
- **Party:** Republican
- **Occupation:** Eagle High School athletics director

## JFAC Role

Succeeded Wendy Horman as House co-chair when Horman resigned to run for Superintendent of Public Instruction (lost in primary). Eagle district is geographically adjacent to Scott Grow's District 14 — both co-chairs from a 15-mile radius of western Ada County controlled by Mike Moyle.

Co-signed the November 2025 letter (with Kelly Anthon) announcing voting rule changes without a committee vote — an improper instrument under JFAC Rule 11.

## Open Meetings Challenge

Co-recipient of CCA letter, March 4, 2026. Statutory response deadline: ~March 18, 2026.
