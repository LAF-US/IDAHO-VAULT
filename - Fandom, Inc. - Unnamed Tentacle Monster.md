This is a creature found in [the Caves of the Void](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Caves_of_the_Void "Caves of the Void").

## Description

This monster has a multitude of tentacles which they uses to catch prey. These tentacles are attached to a gray body consisting of only a large mouth full of sharp teeth.

## History

When [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") and [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") areattacked by [a dog-like monster](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Unnamed_Dog_Monster "Unnamed Dog Monster") in [the caves](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Caves_of_the_Void "Caves of the Void") while looking for [the Sceptre of the Ancients](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sceptre_of_the_Ancients "The Sceptre of the Ancients"), [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") is forced to jump onto one of this creature’s tentacles hanging over a chasm, mistaking it for a vine. [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") pushes [the dog-monster](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Unnamed_Dog_Monster "Unnamed Dog Monster") into the chasm and [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") realises the tentacle is moving. She tries to swing off by it wraps itself around her wrist and pulls her up.

The tentacle pulls her over a ledge and [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") realises she is being dragged towards a gaping mouth. Realising the creature has no eyes, she goes limps and throws a stone at another tentacle, tricking it into directing it attention elsewhere, and manages to escape.
