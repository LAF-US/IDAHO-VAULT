![Three Generals](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Three_Generals.png)

The **Three Generals** were [Mevolent's](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent "Mevolent") top three generals during [the War](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_War "The War"). Vengeous, is now dead, and Lord Vile himself is also technically dead, but only because Skulduggery regained control of himself. Part of [Mevolent's Forces](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent%27s_Forces "Mevolent's Forces"), they largely came into being around the plot, and execution of the said plot, to kill [Skulduggery Pleasant](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant").

Skulduggery kiled Serpine in book 1 but he lived on as a spirit and was resurrected in book 15.

Serpine was killed by the [Sceptre](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Sceptre_of_the_Ancients "Sceptre of the Ancients") in the hands of Skulduggery Pleasant and Baron Vengous was killed in the attack against the [Grotesquery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Grotesquery "Grotesquery").

In [Mevolent's dimension](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent%27s_dimension "Mevolent's dimension"), Serpine turned against Mevolent, leaving Vengeous and Vile as his generals.

## The Generals

### Nefarian Serpine

![Nefarian Serpine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Serpine.png)

Nefarian Serpine

Serpine was the man who killed [Skulduggery Pleasant](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") as well as his wife and child. He possesses a red hand which can inflict immense pain upon a person resulting in death. After the War, Serpine made a truce with [the Sanctuary](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Irish_Sanctuary "The Irish Sanctuary"). In the [first book](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant_\(book\) "Skulduggery Pleasant (book)"), Skulduggery used [The Sceptre of the Ancients](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sceptre_of_the_Ancients "The Sceptre of the Ancients") to kill Serpine after he tried to steal [the Book of Names](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Book_of_Names "The Book of Names"). He was Mevolent's right-hand man.

Known as the most notorious of the Three Generals, Serpine was cunning and insidious and was also known for his back-up plans, which, according to Skulduggery Pleasant was what made him so dangerous. In the third book, we see that Skulduggery is able to defeat Serpine with relative ease in their battle in the caves. However, Serpine has so many little tricks and surprises hidden away, he is able to keep up with very powerful opponents. When he takes control of the Sceptre of the Ancients, he is almost unstoppable.

*"You never could fight worth a damn."* - Skulduggery Pleasant

### Baron Vengeous

![Baron Vengeous](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Baron.png)

Baron Vengeous

After The War ended, Vengeous was imprisoned, but eighty years later was freed by [Billy-Ray Sanguine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Billy-Ray_Sanguine "Billy-Ray Sanguine"). Vengeous attempted to use [the Grotesquery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Grotesquery "The Grotesquery") to bring [the Faceless Ones](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Faceless_Ones "Faceless Ones") back and even managed to obtain [Lord Vile's armour](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Lord_Vile%27s_armour "Lord Vile's armour"). However, his plot was stopped by Skulduggery and Valkyrie before the Grotesquery could complete its mission, Vengeous was shot by Skulduggery and the Grotesquery was destroyed, both by the combined efforts of Valkyrie Cain, Skulduggery Pleasant, Tanith Low and [Mr Bliss](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Bliss "Mr. Bliss").

### Lord Vile

![Lord Vile](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/skulduggery/images/7/76/Lord_Vile_New.JPG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20170418183452)

Lord Vile

Lord Vile was the most powerful general. It was once thought that Lord Vile's [Necromancy](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Necromancy "Necromancy") brought Skulduggery back from the dead, but in reality, Vile is nothing but Skulduggery without his conscience. Since only Skulduggery and [Auron Tenebrae](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Auron_Tenebrae "Auron Tenebrae") knew this, it was thought that Lord Vile had been missing, presumed dead, before the first book. Lord Vile returns when Skulduggery's subconscious animates the armour to kill [Tesseract](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tesseract "Tesseract") and eventually used the armour to become the true Lord Vile again. Vile disappeared during the War after Skulduggery was able to reassert himself.

## Alternate Dimension

![Mevolent, the master of the Three Generals](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/skulduggery/images/2/2f/Mevolent_new.JPG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20170801205645)

Mevolent, the master of the Three Generals

In the alternate dimension, all three generals are dead except Serpine. Vile was destroyed by the Sceptre of the Ancients. Vengeous was infected by drauguar and killed by Skullduggery Pleasant using the God-killer sword. He wanted to die (fair enough he used to be married to Eliza Scorn) and Skullduggery chopped off his head.

## Trivia

- All Three Generals were Necromancers, though Vengeous only practiced the discipline after the War was over, using Vile’s stolen armor.
