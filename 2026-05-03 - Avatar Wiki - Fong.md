General Fong is the Earth Kingdom officer who tries to force Aang into the
Avatar State by engineering extreme emotional pressure. He is the episode's
mechanical antagonist for the trigger experiment.

## Vault Use

This is the coercive-trigger surface for the Avatar State episode.
