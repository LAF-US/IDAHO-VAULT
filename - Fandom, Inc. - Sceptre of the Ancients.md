**The Sceptre of the Ancients**, the Original [God-Killer](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/God-Killer "God-Killer"), was an incredibly powerful tool used by [The Ancients](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ancients "The Ancients") to kill [the Faceless Ones](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Faceless_Ones "Faceless Ones"). It was a golden rod, about the length of a thigh bone and had a [Black Crystal](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Black_Crystal "Black Crystal") embedded into it's hilt. It sang to its owner when someone approached it but stayed quiet when the Ancients stole it. The Sceptre was a focusing tool for the black crystal and shoots out black lightning that turned anything it came into contact with to dust. Once the previous owner had died, all one needed to do take ownership was to touch it.

## History

### Pre-Books

The Sceptre was made by the Faceless Ones to kill the Gods that had fled to Earth following the Great Betrayal. It was powered by [a black crystal](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Black_Crystal "Black Crystal") embedded in its hilt. [The crystal](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Black_Crystal "Black Crystal") was meant to sing to [the Faceless Ones](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Faceless_Ones "Faceless Ones") when an enemy was near, but was silent when it was taken by [the Ancients](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ancients "The Ancients"). Whereas the Dark Gods had fuelled the Sceptre with hate and malice, the [Ancients](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ancients "The Ancients") fuelled it with hope and determination. Using the Sceptre, [the Ancients](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ancients "The Ancients") successfully forced [the Faceless Ones](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Faceless_Ones "Faceless Ones") to retreat into another dimension. Afterwards, [the Ancients](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ancients "The Ancients") began fighting among themselves, and turned the Sceptre against each other, killing all but one of them, a sorcerer called [the Last of the Ancients](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Last_of_the_Ancients "The Last of the Ancients"), who hurled the Sceptre deep into the earth.

In 2006 it was discovered during an archaeological dig. It was bought by [Gordon Edgley](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Gordon_Edgley "Gordon Edgley") who wanted to study it for a book about [the Faceless Ones](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Faceless_Ones "Faceless Ones"). After authenticating it he hid it in the [Caves of the Void](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Caves_of_the_Void "Caves of the Void") beneath [his house](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Gordon_Edgley%27s_house "Gordon Edgley's house"), with the key to [the Caves](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Caves_of_the_Void "Caves of the Void") being [a brooch](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Gordon%E2%80%99s_Brooch "Gordon’s Brooch") he has his sister-in-law [Beryl](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Beryl_Edgley "Beryl Edgley") set to inherit in his will.

![The Sceptre of the Ancients](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/skulduggery/images/7/70/Sceptre_American_cover.JPG/revision/latest?cb=20170512153008)

The Sceptre of the Ancients

[Nefarian Serpine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Nefarian_Serpine "Nefarian Serpine") stole the Sceptre and used it to kill [Eachan Meritorious](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Eachan_Meritorious "Eachan Meritorious") and [Morwenna Crow](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Morwenna_Crow "Morwenna Crow"), and later kills [Sagacious Tome](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Sagacious_Tome "Sagacious Tome") despite him being on his side. The Sceptre was used to kill the Elders so he could get the [Book of Names](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Book_of_Names "Book of Names"), which meant Serpine could rule the world. However, [Skulduggery Pleasant](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") tricked Serpine into using the Sceptre to destroy the Book, and not long after Skulduggery takes it from Serpine and uses it to kill him. The Black Crystal is destroyed after Serpine is killed. China Sorrows was then given the Spectre to add to her obsessive collection of magic.

After being given [the key](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Gordon%E2%80%99s_brooch "Gordon’s brooch") to [the Caves of the Void](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Caves_of_the_Void "Caves of the Void") by [Mr. Bliss](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Bliss "Mr. Bliss"), Serpine entered, and soon found the Sceptre in a wooden chest. Skulduggery appeared and fought him, but Serpine managed to heal any wounds he recieved in seconds. Serpine was about the destroy Skulduggery with the Sceptre when [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") jumped from the shadows and rammed into him, causing him to miss. Skulduggery and Valkyrie fleed Serpine, who pursued them with his [Hollow Men](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Hollow_Man "Hollow Man"). Using the Sceptre he destroyed Skulduggery’s [Canary Car](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Canary_Car "Canary Car") outside [Grimwood House](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Grimwood_House "Grimwood House"), but another car pulled up, and Skulduggery and Valkyrie, joined by [Tanith Low](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tanith_Low "Tanith Low"), got in as it drove away.

### The Faceless Ones

![The Sceptre during ](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/skulduggery/images/f/f4/Sceptre.JPG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20140721143754)

The Sceptre during the Battle of Aranmore Farm

Valkyrie and Skulduggery obtain a new [katahedral crystal](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Katahedral_crystal "Katahedral crystal") for the Sceptre when the Faceless Ones escape through the portal between realities. Valkyrie Cain became the new wielder of it and accidentally destroyed two bookcases that [China Sorrows](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/China_Sorrows "China Sorrows") owned by mistake. The Sceptre was used to successfully kill a Faceless One, but another manages to destroys the Spectre and the Black Crystal within it and in doing so destroyed itself.

### Kingdom Of The Wicked

Another alternate version of the Sceptre was still in perfect condition in the [Parallel Dimension](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Parallel_Dimension "Parallel Dimension") but was in the throne room of [Mevolent's Palace](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent%27s_Palace "Mevolent's Palace"). It was retrieved by Skulduggery and Valkyrie, with the aid of the Serpine in that reality, and taken by China just before they shunt back. During the battle at ratoath mevolent steals it from China and uses it against Darquesse. Darquesse knocks it out of mevolent’s hand and after he pulls her head off and she hits him with a horse Darquesse retrieves the sceptre then china is killed by serpine who attacks Darquesse just as she shunts back to the main dimension. Darquesse looses the sceptre and she thinks it’s still Leibniz dimension. It is then stolen by [Valkyrie Cain's reflection](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain%27s_reflection "Valkyrie Cain's reflection"), unbeknownst to anyone but her.

### Last Stand Of Dead Men

![The sceptre seen in a info book.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/skulduggery/images/d/d3/F6E6BC83-3BAB-42D7-94DE-EF7CEAA35C90.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20210311202152)

The sceptre seen in a info book.

The Reflection still had the Sceptre when the reflection went rogue, and attempted to kill Valkyrie with it. She failed, and didn't use until [the Battle of Roarhaven](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Roarhaven "The Battle of Roarhaven"), when she became an ally of Skulduggery Pleasant. She accidentally allowed [Alice Edgley](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Alison_Edgley "Alison Edgley") to touch the crystal, but it didn't kill her as she was descended from the Ancients. The reflection didn't check to see if the rule applied to reflections.

[Charivari](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Charivari "Charivari") was killed by the reflection using the Sceptre of the Ancients during the [the Battle of Roarhaven](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Roarhaven "The Battle of Roarhaven").

### The Dying of the Light

[Stephanie (Valkyrie Cain's reflection)](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Stephanie_\(Valkyrie_Cain%27s_reflection\) "Stephanie (Valkyrie Cain's reflection)") was still in possession of Sceptre following the events of Last Stand of Dead Men and was using to Hunt down the Renegades (Super-charged Sorcerers) and she also used it in her confrontations with [Darquesse](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Darquesse "Darquesse") and other sorcerers. After Stephanie was murdered by Darquesse, ownership of the Sceptre passed on to [Alice](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Alice_Edgley "Alice Edgley"), as Stephanie hid it under her mattress, and Alice accidentally took ownership.

In order to fight Darquesse, Valkyrie had to somehow take control of the Sceptre. Consequently, she forces herself to kill Alice, so she could take ownership of it, for which she never forgave herself. By the laws of the Sceptre, it bonded to Valkyrie - only to be thrown straight off the planet by Darquesse.

## Seasons of War

In [Seasons of War](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Seasons_of_War "Seasons of War"), it was recovered and Valkyrie Cain uses the Sceptre to defeat hoards off draugar and [necromancers](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Necromancer "Necromancer") and eventually uses it to destroy [Lord Vile](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Lord_Vile "Lord Vile"). At the end of the book, she uses the Sceptre to kill [Mevolent](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent "Mevolent") only to find out that was his reflection and the real Mevolent takes the Sceptre from Valkyrie and snaps in half. The best scientists in [Roarhaven](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Roarhaven_City "Roarhaven City") all say it is beyond repair.

### Dead or Alive

The Sceptre was reconstructed, the Crystal being fitted onto a new handle.
