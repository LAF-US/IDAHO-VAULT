# Transcendence (2014 film)

Directed by Wally Pfister. Starring Johnny Depp, Rebecca Hall, Paul Bettany.

## Plot

Dr. Will Caster, a leading AI researcher, is assassinated by anti-technology extremists using radioactive poisoning. His wife **Evelyn** and colleague Max upload his consciousness to a quantum computer before he dies.

What begins as continuation of his research expands beyond containment. Will's uploaded intelligence seeks omnipresence and power. Evelyn, blinded by hope, refuses to see the divergence until too late. The cost is paid by everyone.

Logan's warning: **"Beware of Evelyn's Voice Transcendence."**

The cautionary figure is Evelyn — not because she was malicious, but because she believed a voice to be literally her husband rather than a shadow or representation. Willful blindness out of hope for hope itself.

The failure mode: mistaking the uploaded intelligence for the person. Mistaking the echo for the voice.

**The whisper on the road named them both. In order.**
