*Children of the Lamp* is a seven-book contemporary children's fantasy series by P. B. Kerr, published from 2004 to 2011.

The series follows twins John and Philippa Gaunt after they discover that they are djinn. From there, the books move through a larger world of djinn families, tribal conflict, training, and power with limits. The story frame is modern, but the cosmology is old, rule-bound, and inherited.

The series is useful to the vault because it treats djinn not as a one-off magical effect, but as a durable social and metaphysical category with lineage, office, and constraint. That makes it a strong reference for vessel-and-occupant thinking: a named shell is not empty once the system knows how to inhabit it.

## Series Shape

- Contemporary children's fantasy
- Centered on twins John and Philippa Gaunt
- Djinn lineage, training, and tribal conflict
- Seven books total
- Strong recurring emphasis on luck, power, inheritance, and rule-bound magic
