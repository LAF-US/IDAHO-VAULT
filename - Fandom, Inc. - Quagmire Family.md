The **Quagmire family** is made up of [Duncan](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Duncan_Quagmire "Duncan Quagmire"), [Isadora](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Isadora_Quagmire "Isadora Quagmire") and [Quigley](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Quigley_Quagmire "Quigley Quagmire") (the triplets) with their parents, [Mr.](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Quagmire "Mr. Quagmire") and [Mrs. Quagmire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Mrs._Quagmire "Mrs. Quagmire").

## Members

- [Mr. Quagmire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Quagmire "Mr. Quagmire") †
- [Mrs. Quagmire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Mrs._Quagmire "Mrs. Quagmire") †
- [Duncan Quagmire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Duncan_Quagmire "Duncan Quagmire") (status unknown; possibly killed by [The Great Unknown](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Great_Unknown "The Great Unknown"))
- [Isadora Quagmire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Isadora_Quagmire "Isadora Quagmire") (status unknown; possibly killed by [The Great Unknown](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Great_Unknown "The Great Unknown"))
- [Quigley Quagmire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Quigley_Quagmire "Quigley Quagmire") (status unknown; possibly killed by [The Great Unknown](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Great_Unknown "The Great Unknown"))

They originally lived in the [Quagmire Mansion](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Quagmire_Mansion "Quagmire Mansion"), and were very wealthy due to the [Quagmire Sapphires](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Quagmire_Sapphires "Quagmire Sapphires"). They seem to be a [VFD](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Volunteer_Fire_Department "Volunteer Fire Department") family, as claimed by [Jacques Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Jacques_Snicket "Jacques Snicket") to [Quigley Quagmire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Quigley_Quagmire "Quigley Quagmire").[^1] (In the Netflix series, the Quagmire family are unambiguously part of VFD.) [^2] [^3]

[Their mansion](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Quagmire_Mansion "Quagmire Mansion") was burnt down in [a fire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Quagmire_Fire "Quagmire Fire") which killed both Quagmire parents. According to *[The Daily Punctilio](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Daily_Punctilio "The Daily Punctilio")*, the Quagmire estate is currently under care of [Esmé Squalor](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Esm%C3%A9_Squalor "Esmé Squalor").[^1] It is unknown if Esmé still holds control of the estate due to the question of her survival in the [Hotel Denouement fire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Hotel_Denouement_fire "Hotel Denouement fire").

### Speculation

It is often speculated that the Quagmires are related to the [Denouement Family](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Denouement_Family "Denouement Family"); [Dewey Denouement](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Dewey_Denouement "Dewey Denouement") says that triplets run in his family.[^4] If the Quagmires and Denouements are related, it is likely through Mrs. Quagmire, as the gene for carrying triplets is passed down through the mother.

## Netflix Series Divergent Canon

In the Netflix adaptation of *[A Series of Unfortunate Events](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/A_Series_of_Unfortunate_Events_\(TV_series\) "A Series of Unfortunate Events (TV series)")*, a mysterious husband and wife credited as only "Mother" and "Father" appear throughout the season. For most of the episodes the audience are led to believe through various clues that they are the [Baudelaire parents](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Baudelaire_Family "Baudelaire Family"), having survived the fire and subsequently been kidnapped.

In " [The Miserable Mill: Part One](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Miserable_Mill:_Part_One "The Miserable Mill: Part One") ", after arriving at a door identical to the one that the Baudelaires wait on the inside of in the [Lucky Smells Lumbermill](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Lucky_Smells_Lumbermill "Lucky Smells Lumbermill"), the parents are revealed to be the parents not of the Baudelaires but of the Quagmires, therefore identifying them as "Mr. and Mrs. Quagmire".

When [Count Olaf](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Olaf "Count Olaf") meets Duncan and Isadora at [Prufrock Preparatory School](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Prufrock_Preparatory_School "Prufrock Preparatory School"), Duncan calls him a miscreant. Olaf immediately remembers about Mrs. Quagmire and calls her a tiresome woman telling the two of three triplets how she once told him 'You miscreant, never again will you darken the doors of this hangar.'.

## Trivia

- "Quagmire" was likely chosen for these characters' surname due to its definition of "a position or situation which is unpleasant or hazardous; *esp.* one from which it is difficult to extricate oneself." It also can mean "an area of wet, boggy land that gives way under foot; a quaking bog," or "something soft, flabby, or yielding." [^5]
- Their most used fanmade names are Karen (Mrs. Quagmire) and Quinten (Mr. Quagmire).
- According to the Netflix series, Mrs. Quagmire calls bad people *miscreants*. [^6]
	- She is also known for reading Italian poetry and activating trap doors. [^6]
- This family’s set of identical triplets are the only set in the franchise confirmed to be triplets from the time of their introduction- all other sets were initially thought to only be twins before they were revealed to be triplets later on in the storyline, a recurring theme in the series.
