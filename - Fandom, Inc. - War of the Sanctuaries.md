**The War of the Sanctuaries** is a conflict between twenty-six [Sanctuaries](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Sanctuary "Sanctuary") around the world, fought in [2013](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant:_Last_Stand_of_Dead_Men "Skulduggery Pleasant: Last Stand of Dead Men") from August to October.

Tensions between the [Supreme Council](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Supreme_Council "Supreme Council") and the [Irish Sanctuary](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Irish_Sanctuary "Irish Sanctuary") grow for years, and war is finally declared after the murder of [Bernard Sult](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Bernard_Sult "Bernard Sult") by the [Children of the Spider](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Children_of_the_Spider "Children of the Spider"), who attribute this action to the Irish Sanctuary. The Sanctuaries of the other [Cradles of Magic](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Cradle_of_Magic "Cradle of Magic"), [Africa](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/African_Sanctuary "African Sanctuary") and [Australia](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Australian_Sanctuary "Australian Sanctuary"), soon join the war on Ireland's behalf.

Twenty-one Sanctuaries against three Cradles is close to an even match, until the Cradles suffer a devastating loss at the [Battle of the Keep](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_the_Keep "Battle of the Keep"). Shortly afterwards, a treaty is signed, and both sides ally against the growing threat of the [Warlocks](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Warlock "Warlock").

## Main Events

- Defensive Shields are raised around [Ireland](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Ireland "Ireland").
- The Supreme Council ordered [Mantis](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mantis "Mantis") to find a way in. He uses the [Midnight Hotel](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Midnight_Hotel "The Midnight Hotel").
- [Tanith Low](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tanith_Low "Tanith Low") and the [Monster Hunters](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Monster_Hunters "The Monster Hunters") attempt to disable the hotel. Mantis' forces get through.
- [The Dead Men](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Dead_Men "The Dead Men") go to France to reclain [The Engineer](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Engineer "The Engineer"). Australia and Africa joins the war on Ireland's side to protect their own interests.
- Mantis was led into a trap at an abandoned fort, but the preemptive attack went wrong when [Dusk](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Dusk "Dusk") murdered [Moloch](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Moloch "Moloch"), the only one who could control [Skulduggery Pleasant](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") 's reinforcements: the [vampires](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire "Vampire").
- Mantis pushed the Irish mages back and captured or killed many. [Valkyrie Cain](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") was interrogated until she escaped.
- [Fletcher Renn](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Fletcher_Renn "Fletcher Renn") was captured by [witches](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Brides_of_Blood_Tears "Brides of Blood Tears") and trapped in Africa. Skulduggery and Valkyrie went to free him but Valkyrie succumbed to her [inner self](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain#Darquesse "Valkyrie Cain") and disappeared for weeks.
- [Ghastly Bespoke](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Ghastly_Bespoke "Ghastly Bespoke") and [Anton Shudder](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Anton_Shudder "Anton Shudder") are killed by the [Man with the Golden Eyes](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Man_with_Golden_Eyes "The Man with Golden Eyes").
- The Man with Golden Eyes' plan goes wrong when [Charivari](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Charivari "Charivari") and his forces attacked [Roarhaven](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Roarhaven "Roarhaven"). The following [battle](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Roarhaven "The Battle of Roarhaven") results in the collapse of the [Supreme Council](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Supreme_Council "Supreme Council").
- [The Accelerator](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Accelerator "The Accelerator") goes into its final phase and would destroy the world unless a soul, willingly given, was sacrificed.

## Outcomes

- Ireland managed to prevent another catastrophe.
- Darquesse saved [China Sorrows](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/China_Sorrows "China Sorrows") from death, tortured [Erskine Ravel](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Erskine_Ravel "Erskine Ravel") and departed.
- Skulduggery Pleasant asks [Valkyrie Cain's reflection](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain%27s_reflection "Valkyrie Cain's reflection") to help him hunt Darquesse down and kill her, if necessary.
- China is elected [Grand Mage](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Grand_Mage "Grand Mage") of Ireland.
- Many Sanctuaries lose a member of their council.
