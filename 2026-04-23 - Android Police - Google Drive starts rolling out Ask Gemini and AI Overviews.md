Google Gemini features have been available in some form across Workspace apps, such as Drive, for quite some time now. In a Workspace-related announcement last month, Google said it would incorporate [**AI Overviews** and **Ask Gemini**](https://www.androidpolice.com/new-gemini-features-in-docs-sheets-slides-and-drive/) into the cloud storage app.

It has taken a few weeks, but that day appears to be nearly here. In separate announcements on the *Workspace Updates Blog*, Google says **[AI Overviews](https://workspaceupdates.googleblog.com/2026/04/ai-overviews-in-drive-now-generally-available.html)** and **[Ask Gemini in Drive](https://workspaceupdates.googleblog.com/2026/04/ask-gemini-in-drive-now-generally-available.html)** are "generally" available to users, albeit with some caveats, which we'll get to later. The rollout comes after both features were introduced to Google Drive in March as part of a limited beta.

People familiar with Google Search will be aware of **AI Overviews** and [some of its past blunders](https://www.androidpolice.com/im-finally-ditching-google-search-after-its-latest-ai-experiment/). Thankfully, it makes a great deal of sense for an app like Google Drive, as it delivers an immediate summary of your query directly from the search bar. Google positions this as a way to get specific questions answered about files without manually scouring through each file for details.

## 'Ask Gemini' assists with more complex tasks

![Ask Gemini in Drive](https://static0.anpoimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/ask-gemini-in-drive.jpeg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)

Credit: Google

*Ask Gemini*

While they may seem similar, **Ask Gemini** offers a more advanced setup. Google intends it to serve as an alternative to **AI Overviews** in Drive, especially for more complicated queries. **Ask Gemini** is also recommended for users who want to save their conversations with the chatbot for future reference.

Furthermore, users also gain access to *Drive projects* with **Ask Gemini**. This is a collaboration-oriented feature that brings together the important emails and files in one place, while giving Gemini access to all this information for a free-flowing conversation.

On the other hand, if you have a more direct query, like " *What date was the \*\*\* invoice from*," the **AI Overviews** in Drive should get the job done. Google is also making **Ask Gemini** and **AI Overview** available in all 29 languages that are accessible from the Gemini side panel.

These AI features in Google Drive will be available to users on the Business Standard/Plus, Enterprise Standard/Plus plans, the Google AI Pro for Education add-on, and Google AI Pro and Ultra subscribers.

As with most Workspace features, the rollout pace will vary depending on the release schedule, with **Ask Gemini** in English beginning to roll out as of yesterday, *April 22.* It should appear to most eligible users by early or mid-May. However, support for the 28 additional languages isn't expected until early May, and may not be widely available until later that month.

**AI Overviews** in Google Drive will follow a similar release timeline, starting with English and rolling out support for the remaining 28 languages by the end of May.
