**Imps** are the lowest type of [demon](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Demons "Demons"), and are generally quite rude, thus only performing well as a messenger, or in large numbers of them. Imps are commonly also trapped in reflective surfaces to create scrying glasses.

Like all spirits, imps possess some form of [magic](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic"), but it is extremely weak compared to the others. Unlike other demons, they are unable to change form and thus gain respite from the pain the Earth's pull causes on their [essence](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Essence "Essence"). As the lowest basic type of spirit, they are easily summoned by even mildly-competent [magicians](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Magicians "Magicians") in large numbers.

[Nathaniel](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Nathaniel "Nathaniel") used an imp that took the form of baby in his personal scrying glass. Another imp was carrying mail from [Simon Lovelace](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Simon_Lovelace "Simon Lovelace") when [Bartimaeus](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Bartimaeus "Bartimaeus") detained him and proceeded to go through the said mail whilst forcing the imp to call him by a different glorifying title every time he was addressed.
