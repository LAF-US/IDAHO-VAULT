**Kibeth** is the third of the [Nine Bright Shiners](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Nine_Bright_Shiners "Nine Bright Shiners"). In [The Beginning](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/The_Beginning "The Beginning"), Kibeth became part of [the Charter](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/The_Charter "The Charter"), but some part of her remained in the [Old Kingdom](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Kingdom "Old Kingdom") as the [Disreputable Dog](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Disreputable_Dog "Disreputable Dog"). As a [bell](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Bells "Bells"), Kibeth is the Walker, which can make others go where the wielder wishes. The Disreputable Dog stood for herself at the binding of [Orannis](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Orannis "Orannis").

## Bell Sound

Kibeth has several sounds, which can be tricky for the wielder. Rung properly (backwards, forwards, and then in a figure eight), the peals are all different from each other, but together make a little marching tune, a dancing song, a parade.[^1]

## Trivia

- Kibeth is one of two Charter creators whose remnants are encountered (the other being [Astarael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Astarael "Astarael")).
- [Lirael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Lirael "Lirael") finds herself automatically reaching for Kibeth whenever she needs a bell, because of her friendship with the Disreputable Dog.
- Kibeth is the second-most-used instrument in the series, behind [Saraneth](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Saraneth "Saraneth") (which often precedes it).
- As the Disreputable Dog is female, it can be implied Kibeth was as well.
- Kibeth is one of three bells [Idrach](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Idrach "Idrach") forges in [Death](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death").
