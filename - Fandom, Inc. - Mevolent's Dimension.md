**Mevolent's dimension**, also known as the **Leibniz Dimension** or **Dimension X**, is an alternate dimenision that has followed the same [timeline](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline "Timeline") as [Skulduggery Pleasant's dimension](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant_universe "Skulduggery Pleasant universe") up to a certain point in the past. It is unknown how much has changed but it is shown that [Mevolent](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent "Mevolent") is not only alive but has achieved victory in [the war](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_War "The War"), with his palace replacing Ireland's [Sanctuary](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Sanctuary "Sanctuary"). Mevolent's victory is most likely because in this dimension [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") is still [Lord Vile](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Lord_Vile "Lord Vile").

## History

### The Purges

[Mevolent](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent "Mevolent") takes control of the dimension and starts his Purges. Those who refused to bow down to him were ultimately killed, and the warlocks have been virtually extinct due to that. Shortly after that,[China Sorrows](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/China_Sorrows "China Sorrows") led the revolution against [Mevolent](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent "Mevolent"). Other members of this [revolution](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Resistance "The Resistance") included [Dexter Vex](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Dexter_Vex "Dexter Vex"), [Anton Shudder](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Anton_Shudder "Anton Shudder"), and [Ghastly Bespoke](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Ghastly_Bespoke "Ghastly Bespoke"). Their main concern is not to protect the mortals, but to overthrow Mevolent.

[Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") repeatedly shifted between this dimension and her own because of the echoing shunt the [Dimensional Shunter](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Dimensional_Shunter "Dimensional Shunter"), [Silas Nadir](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Silas_Nadir "Silas Nadir") cast on her. Through her few visits, it is revealed that the mortals are terrified of sorcerers in general, and it is as though mortal technology has stopped improving sometime ago, giving the place an old time-y feel. Also, brown seems to be the only color available for garments.

### Downfall of the Resistance

On [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") 's last visit, [Mevolent](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent "Mevolent") managed to hunt down the [The Resistance](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Resistance "The Resistance"). They were defeated when [China Sorrows](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/China_Sorrows "China Sorrows") lost control of the only weapon capable of defeating him: [The Sceptre of the Ancients.](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sceptre_of_the_Ancients "The Sceptre of the Ancients") China was then killed by [Neferian Serpine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Nefarian_Serpine "Nefarian Serpine"). This was known as [The Battle of Ratoath](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Ratoath "The Battle of Ratoath").

After this, [Serpine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Nefarian_Serpine "Nefarian Serpine") took control of [the Resistance](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Resistance "The Resistance") and possibly murdered dimension's version of [Anton Shudder](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Anton_Shudder "Anton Shudder"), he later rescued [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") from Professor [Nye](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Nye "Nye") after she was captured following a confrontation between Mevolent, Lord Vile, [Darquesse](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Darquesse "Darquesse"), [Baron Vengeous](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Baron_Vengeous "Baron Vengeous"), Skulduggery and [Erskine Ravel](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Erskine_Ravel "Erskine Ravel").

With [Serpine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Nefarian_Serpine "Nefarian Serpine") and [Emmertt Pergine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Emmett_Peregrine "Emmett Peregrine"), [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") was able to take the the [Magic-Sucker](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Magic-Sucker "Magic-Sucker") from [Mevolent](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent "Mevolent") and rescue [Erskine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Erskine_Ravel "Erskine Ravel") and [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") from torture, after attempting to steal the gun from [The Resistance](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Resistance "The Resistance"), after been caught,Serpine warned the three to never encounter The Resistance again,or be killed on sight.

## Locations

### Mevolent's Palace

The Palace in which Mevolent lives. It is here that his guards are ordered to kill him every day and assist his rebirth, in order for him to master death. It is also where the God-Killer sword and Sceptre of the Ancients are kept.

### Ratoath

A town in the county of Meath, where The Battle of Ratoath took place.

### The Temple of the Spider

A temple where [The Children of the Spider](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Children_of_the_Spider "The Children of the Spider") practice their beliefs, mentioned in the final book.

### Dublin-Within-the-Wall

The alternate reality's version of Dublin, where the Mevolent's Palace is located and where most of the sorcerers and followers of the Faceless Ones live. The city's actual name is unknown, but during her dimensional jaunts, Dublin-Within-the-Wall is how Valkyrie referred to it.

## Trivia

- This is the 2nd alternative dimension to feature in the series,the first being the The Faceless Ones Dimension.
- It is not confirmed whether this dimension branched off from the Skulduggery Pleasant's Dimension or ran parallel to it from the start. This implication would mean that at least two races of faceless ones exist in the multiverse if this dimension didn't branch off from the prime universe.
- At least two or more members of [The Diablerie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Diablerie "The Diablerie") are alive in this dimension, as they were referred to in present tense by [Mevolent](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent "Mevolent") in *[Kingdom of the Wicked](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant:_Kingdom_of_the_Wicked "Skulduggery Pleasant: Kingdom of the Wicked")* and have not been mentioned since.
- Out of [The Dead Men](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Dead_Men "The Dead Men") in this dimension,the status of Dexter Vex, [Hopeless](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Hopeless "Hopeless"), Erskine Ravel and [Saracen Rue](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Saracen_Rue "Saracen Rue") remain unknown. Out of these three, Dexter Vex was last seen loosing an arm to a redhood in the costly battle for The Resistance and is most likely is dead. Hopeless may have shared the fate of the main dimension counterpart. Saracen Rue's fate is a mystery, but would have mostly have stuck with Dexter and Ghastly if he was alive. Erskine Ravel's may have been tortured to death in this timeline,if [The Torment](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Torment "The Torment") never been able to rescue him or was handed to Mevolent's forces by [The Children of the Spider](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Children_of_the_Spider "The Children of the Spider").
