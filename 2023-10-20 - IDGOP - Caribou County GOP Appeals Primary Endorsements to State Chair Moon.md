**Caribou County GOP Appeals Bonneville’s 2022 Primary Activity** 
(Soda Springs, ID) – Citing concerns that the Bonneville County GOP’s 2022 Republican Primary activism harmed conduct of a free and fair primary election election in Caribou County, the Caribou County GOP today appealed to State GOP Chairwoman Dorothy Moon seeking her intervention to prevent future election tampering. 

The seven-page appeal includes exhibits and testimony showing how the Bonneville GOP’s campaign materials were distributed into Caribou County with intent to influence the nominating process.  A delegation of Caribou Republicans presented their concerns to the Bonneville County Republican Central Committee nearly a year ago but received no commitment to change their practice.  Today’s appeal ensures the matter can be resolved prior to the 2024 Primary. 

“Chairwoman Dorothy Moon’s name appears on the campaign literature Bonneville Republicans produced and allowed to be distributed in Caribou County,” explained Caribou Republican officer Trent Clark.  “Nevertheless, we expect she will see that such predatory campaign activity is destructive to a cohesive and effective statewide political organization.”
