**Rustica Strife** is the mother of [Ghastly Bespoke](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Ghastly_Bespoke "Ghastly Bespoke"). When she was pregnant with him she was cursed. As a result Ghastly was born with hideous scars over his face.

She was a boxer who, according to [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant"), once knocked a man's head clean off. She met her death at the hands of [Lord Vile](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Lord_Vile "Lord Vile"), whom she had attempted to defeat single-handedly. Her boxing being known as legendary, she's won 23 times consecutively. It is a trait she has passed on to Ghastly, and one that he particularly benefited from when he was younger, when made fun of his scars.

It is also mentioned in *[The Faceless Ones](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Faceless_Ones "The Faceless Ones")*, that she defeated [Serpine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Serpine "Serpine") and [Baron Vengeous](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Baron_Vengeous "Baron Vengeous"), and some of [Mevolent's](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent "Mevolent") best men just turned and ran. Ghastly's mother was also a [Sensitive](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Sensitive "Sensitive"), particularly seeing the future. However, it was a gift she didn't want. One of her visions was that of a [dark haired girl](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Stephanie_\(Valkyrie_Cain%27s_reflection\) "Stephanie (Valkyrie Cain's reflection)") joining Skulduggery and dying horribly, a vision fulfilled in *[The Dying of the Light](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Dying_of_the_Light "The Dying of the Light")*.

In *[Last Stand Of Dead Men](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Last_Stand_Of_Dead_Men "Last Stand Of Dead Men")*, Ghastly mentions that his mother had often badgered Ghastly to find a nice girl.

She is a minor character in [Hell Breaks Loose](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant:_Hell_Breaks_Loose "Skulduggery Pleasant: Hell Breaks Loose") (which is where her name is revealed) and in chapter 10 she is described as "undoubtedly the single most feared general in [Meritorious](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Eachan_Meritorious "Eachan Meritorious") ' army".
