The lamp page defines the vessel side of the series' metaphysics.

A lamp is a djinn's home and resting place. The interior may be decorated with personal objects and identity markers. Release from a lamp carries the traditional obligation to grant three wishes under the Baghdad rules.

The important vault takeaway is that the lamp is a named vessel with rules, not a generic container.
