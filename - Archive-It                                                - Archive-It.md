## The leading service for preserving and accessing digital cultural heritage

![Archive-It dashboard and crawl report](https://media.archive-it.org/ait-public-site/images/Southfield_University_crawl_repo_o1SOS6h.format-avif.width-2000.avif)

## Products

![Archive-It's logo](https://media.archive-it.org/ait-public-site/images/Archive-It_navy_no_text.format-avif.max-50x50.avif)

### Web Archiving

Archive-It is our user-controlled, web-based service for creating curated, publicly accessible web archives and born-digital collections.

[Learn about Archive-It](https://archive-it.org/archive-it/)

![ARCH logo in navy](https://media.archive-it.org/ait-public-site/images/Arch_antK1Ji.format-avif.max-50x50.avif)

### Text & Data Mining

ARCH is our research and data processing service that helps users easily build, analyze and study large digital collections computationally and create datasets and data visualizations.

[Learn about ARCH](https://archive-it.org/arch/)

![Vault logo in navy](https://media.archive-it.org/ait-public-site/images/Vault_navy_no_text.format-avif.max-50x50.avif)

### Digital Preservation

Vault is our low-cost, easy-to-use digital repository and preservation service to store, manage, and preserve digital files and collections.

[Learn about Vault](https://archive-it.org/vault/)

[Learn more about other products](https://archive-it.org/products)
