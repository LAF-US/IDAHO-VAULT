The djinn page gives the series' internal species and rule system.

Djinn are beings made of smokeless fire. They have temperature sensitivity when young, are tied to luck and fate, and live inside a wider social order that includes tribes, powers, and a Blue Djinn office. Their abilities are not free-floating magic; they are bounded by inheritance, age, and law.

The important vault takeaway is that djinn are not just wish machines. They are a structured population with biology, politics, and constraint.

## Vault Use

This source supports the vault's understanding of the inhabitant side of the lamp/genie model.
