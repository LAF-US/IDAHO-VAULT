**The Midnight Hotel** is a building that never stays in one location for too long. It travels the world spending a certain amount of time, 12 hours, at every location (The Midnight Hotel is the most well-known example of a Cursion structure: a building that moves). The Hotel is said a place for people to stay if they are in a bad situation. It is owned by [Anton Shudder](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Anton_Shudder "Anton Shudder"), who allows anyone to stay in the hotel. If someone is attacked in The Midnight Hotel, Shudder will fight for the victim of the attack. The Hotel has a total of twenty-four rooms, one of which is used as the holding to place for two thousand [Remnants](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Remnant "Remnant"). This room, Room 24, can only be opened with Anton Shudder's key.

Anton built the Hotel after being dissatisfied with the Sanctuaries around the world. He felt the Sanctuaries were growing too powerful and bureaucratic. He built the Hotel as a refuge for those who operated outside of official boundaries. Sometimes the guests were outlaws and criminals.

After Anton's death, the hotel was passed to a new owner.[^1]

In Midnight it is in possesion of Cadaverous Gant who took it from Anton's ex-boyfriend after killing him.

## Growing A New Hotel2

this extract from the Skulduggery Pleasant Grimoire says that *"Seeds must be harvested from the bushes that grow at the base of the hotel. Once these seeds are planted in a new location and watered, the hotel sprouts and grows in a matter of minutes. The hotel occupants are instantly transported into it as they would in a normal growth cycle, although the first sprouting of a new hotel is a slower process, so they get to witness the somewhat jarring effects from the inside as it grows to full size".* please note that this process is only required for growing a completely new hotel and not for a regular growth cycle.

## Clock

The clock in The Midnight Hotel is located on the second floor and is the device responsible for the hotel changing location every 12 hours

## In Books

### Dark Days

![, the Hotel's owner](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/AntonShudder.png)

Anton Shudder, the Hotel's owner

[Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") and [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") visit the hotel after they think that a member of [the Revengers' Club](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Revengers%27_Club "The Revengers' Club") would use a [Soul Catcher](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Soul_Catcher "Soul Catcher") to steal a [Remnant](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Remnant "Remnant"). Shudder allows the two to stay in the hotel for the night. [Billy-Ray Sanguine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Billy-Ray_Sanguine "Billy-Ray Sanguine") walks into the hotel, and when Skulduggery threatens him, Shudder confirms Sanguine as a guest, which doesn't allow Skulduggery to hurt him.

Later, Sanguine tells the the three of his plans to steal a Remnant. He says that a [Zombie Horde](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Zombie_Horde "Zombie Horde"),led by [Vaurien Scapegrace](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Vaurien_Scapegrace "Vaurien Scapegrace"), are going to attack the hotel. However, Scapegrace calls and tells Sanguine that the [Zombies](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Zombie "Zombie") tasted flesh and had become rabid killers. Anton Shudder brings two volunteers, [Miss Nuncio](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Miss_Nuncio "Miss Nuncio") and [Mr. Jib](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Jib "Mr. Jib"). The three, including Skulduggery, Valkyrie and Sanguine are forced to protect themselves against the horde. Miss Nuncio and Mr. Jib are both killed by the Zombies. After putting up a huge fight, the Zombies are defeated. But Sanguine manages to steal Shudder's key and a [Remnant](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Remnant "Remnant").

After [Caelan's](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Caelan "Caelan") home is destroyed, Valkyrie asks if Shudder would let Caelan stay at the hotel, and he agrees, but his room would be locked during the night for the protection of other guests. The Remnant was retrieved, but was not returned to the hotel. It was kept by the [Necromancers](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Necromancer "Necromancer").

### Mortal Coil

The [High Priest](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/High_Priest "High Priest") [Auron Tenebrae](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Auron_Tenebrae "Auron Tenebrae") told [Solomon Wreath](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Solomon_Wreath "Solomon Wreath") to use the Remnant on a [Sensitive](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Sensitive "Sensitive"), [Finbar Wrong](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Finbar_Wrong "Finbar Wrong"). This led to Anton Shudder to be possessed by a Remnant after he leaves the hotel to buy suppliances and furniture (after Caelan destroyed his room). Shudder releases all the Remnants, which take over [Ireland](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Ireland "Ireland").

It is unknown whether the Remnants would stay in the [Receptacle](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Receptacle "Receptacle") (where they were captured) or if they would be returned to the hotel.

Cadaverous Gant takes control of the abandoned Midnight Hotel and turns into a vast kingdom on the inside. He captures [Alison Edgley](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Alison_Edgley "Alison Edgley"), Valkyrie's little sister, and lures Valkyrie there by threatening to kill Alice.
