Mites are a type of lesser spirit

**Lesser Spirits** are spirits that are weaker then even imps. [Magicians](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Magicians "Magicians") very rarely bother to summon these spirits since there are near useless due to their weakness. an example of lesser spirits are mites a small bug like creature capable of only stinging foes.
