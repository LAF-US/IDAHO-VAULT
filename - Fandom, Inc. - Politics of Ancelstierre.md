Very little of the **Politics of Ancelstierre** is revealed in the [books](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Kingdom_series "Old Kingdom series") published so far. The country's political leader is known as the [Chief Minister](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Chief_Minister "Chief Minister"); the only named occupant of this position is [Edward Sayre](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Edward_Sayre "Edward Sayre"). The parliament of [Ancelstierre](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Ancelstierre "Ancelstierre") is referred to as a **Moot**. Ancelstierre seems to be a parliamentary democracy with a constitutional monarchy.

During the events of *[Lirael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Lirael_\(book\) "Lirael (book)")* and *[Abhorsen](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Abhorsen_\(book\) "Abhorsen (book)")*, [Hedge](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Hedge "Hedge") stirs up unrest - perhaps even war - in countries that Ancelstierre borders. This leads to huge immigration of [Southerlings](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Southerlings "Southerlings"), which in turn leads to increased support of a Fascist political party lead by a man named [Corolini](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Corolini "Corolini"). During an attempted coup, Corolini's thugs try to assassinate the [king](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Torrigan "Torrigan") and [queen](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Sabriel "Sabriel") of the [Old Kingdom](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Kingdom "Old Kingdom").

Corolini's party is the only known political party; the actual policies or opinions of Edward Sayre, or any other politicians, are unknown at this point.
