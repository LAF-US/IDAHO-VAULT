Aang is the current Avatar in the cycle described by the episode. The page
shows him as the living bridge between the current world and the accumulated
memory of previous Avatars. He is the active instance that can enter the Avatar
State, connect with past lives, and in the episode be forced toward that state
by General Fong.

## Vault Use

This is the active-instance surface for the cycle.
