Katara is the waterbender whose apparent death is used to force Aang into the
Avatar State in the episode. More broadly, she is one of Aang's closest
companions and one of the people whose safety is tied to his emotional
regulation and Avatar State trigger conditions.

## Vault Use

This is the emotional trigger surface in the episode mechanics.
