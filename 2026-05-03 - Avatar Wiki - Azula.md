Azula is the Fire Nation antagonist whose lightning strike later in the series
severs Aang's connection to his past lives. In the Avatar State page, she is
part of the same mechanical field of coercion, attack, and cycle-breaking
danger.

## Vault Use

This is the cycle-severance threat surface adjacent to the Avatar State.
