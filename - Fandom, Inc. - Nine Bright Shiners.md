The **Nine Bright Shiners** are incredibly powerful [Free Magic](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Free_Magic "Free Magic") beings who were the first to develop conscious thought. This rare sentience gave them dominion over other [Free Magic creatures](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Free_Magic_Creatures "Free Magic Creatures"), who refer to them as "highest of the high." [^1]

The Nine Bright Shiners are:

1. [Ranna](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Ranna "Ranna")
2. [Mosrael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Mosrael "Mosrael")
3. [Kibeth](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Kibeth "Kibeth")
4. [Dyrim](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Dyrim "Dyrim")
5. [Belgaer](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Belgaer "Belgaer")
6. [Saraneth](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Saraneth "Saraneth")
7. [Astarael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Astarael "Astarael")
8. [Yrael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Yrael "Yrael")
9. [Orannis](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Orannis "Orannis")

The first Seven of the Bright Shiners sought to use their strength to create Life independently capable of existing. However, they were opposed by [Orannis](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Orannis "Orannis"), The Destroyer, the strongest of the Nine Bright Shiners. He sought to counter all creation with destruction, and annihilated the Life that had begun on six previous worlds. Finally, the Seven Shiners stood together and bound him to stop him once and for all [^2], but their binding starts to fail at the beginning of [*Lirael*](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Lirael:_Daughter_of_the_Clayr "Lirael: Daughter of the Clayr").

When [Lirael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Lirael "Lirael") uses the [Dark Mirror](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_mirror "Dark mirror") to see how the original binding was performed, she sees the Seven confront Orannis on the seventh planet he intended to annihilate (that of the [Old Kingdom](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Kingdom "Old Kingdom")). Upon the standoff between the two forces, the Eighth Bright Shiner [Yrael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Yrael "Yrael") hid rather than take sides, but he was captured and made a servant of the [Abhorsens](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Abhorsen "Abhorsen"): [Mogget](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Mogget "Mogget").

After the binding of [Orannis](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Orannis "Orannis"), during which the Seven made the [Charter](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/The_Charter "The Charter"), they transformed themselves into physical objects and flesh-and-blood: two are in [The Great Charter Stones](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Great_Charter_Stones "Great Charter Stones") and the [Wall](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/The_Wall "The Wall"), while five are in various bloodlines (the [Wallmakers](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Wallmaker "Wallmaker"), the [Clayr](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Clayr "Clayr"), the [Royal line](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_line "Royal line"), and the Abhorsens, not necessarily one each).[^3]

[Kibeth](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Kibeth "Kibeth") and [Astarael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Astarael "Astarael") are known to have lost only some power to the Charter and lived on as the [Disreputable Dog](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Disreputable_Dog "Disreputable Dog") and in the well beneath [Abhorsen's House](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Abhorsen%27s_House "Abhorsen's House") respectively; their forms (a female dog and an impossibly tall, weeping woman) suggest both Shiners were female. [Mogget/Yrael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Yrael "Yrael") is male and refers to [Ranna](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Ranna "Ranna") as "she," [^4] while [Orannis](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Orannis "Orannis") is called "he" throughout the books. The genders of the other Shiners are not known.

Currently, [Orannis](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Orannis "Orannis") remains bound in two metal hemispheres buried beneath the earth. His second binding nearly failed, and was only possible with the last-moment power of [Mogget/Yrael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Yrael "Yrael") (freed by [Sameth](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Sameth "Sameth")), who chose to oppose him this time. As such, he was given his freedom and the entirety of his identity was restored by Sabriel wielding Belgaer the end of [*Abhorsen*](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Abhorsen_\(Book\) "Abhorsen (Book)"). No longer bound, he still occasionally visits (usually Sameth) in his cat shape.

The [Disreputable Dog/Kibeth](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Kibeth "Kibeth") was "killed" in some capacity when she severed [Lirael's](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Lirael "Lirael") [hand](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Goldenhand "Goldenhand") to absorb Orannis's final destructive power in her stead. While she is no longer [Lirael's](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Lirael "Lirael") constant companion in [Life](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life"), at the end of *[Goldenhand](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Goldenhand_\(Book\) "Goldenhand (Book)")* she assisted [Lirael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Lirael "Lirael") and [Nicholas Sayre](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Nicholas_Sayre "Nicholas Sayre") in finally defeating [Chlorr of the Mask](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Chlorr_of_the_Mask "Chlorr of the Mask") / [Clariel](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Clariel "Clariel") in [Death](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death").

The power of the Seven exists in the necromantic [bells](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Bells "Bells") and [panpipes](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Panpipes "Panpipes"), which carry their names. In addition, they were (and perhaps still are) used to name the days of the week, although only four are mentioned in [*Clariel*](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Clariel:_The_Lost_Abhorsen "Clariel: The Lost Abhorsen"): Bethday ([Kibeth](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Kibeth "Kibeth")), Dyrmday ([Dyrim](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Dyrim "Dyrim")), Belday ([Belgaer](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Belgaer "Belgaer")), and Starday ([Astarael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Astarael "Astarael")).
