**Ghastly Bespoke** was a sorcerer and tailor that could get the measurements of a customer from one glance. He is notable for serving on the [Dead Men](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Dead_Men "Dead Men") during the war against [Mevolent](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent "Mevolent"). Later he became an Elder of the [Irish Sanctuary](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Irish_Sanctuary "The Irish Sanctuary") which he served on until being betrayed and murdered by former teammate and friend [Erskine Ravel](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Erskine_Ravel "Erskine Ravel").

## Biography

### History

When [Ghastly Bespoke's mother](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Ghastly_Bespoke%27s_mother "Ghastly Bespoke's mother") was pregnant with him, she was jinxed. Because of the curse, Ghastly had scars covered his whole head. Ghastly has tried many attempts to eliminate the scars but has failed to do so, but he received a facade tattoo from China to cover them up. Due to his rather hideous state this has apparently eliminated any chance in the "normal", non-magic world. Ever since he was a teenager, he had been friends with [Skulduggery Pleasant](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant"), who was around the same age as him.

Although Ghastly continuously states that he received his love of boxing from his father and his love of sewing from his mother, it is actually the other way around. Ghastly's mother was a professional boxer that fought [Nefarian Serpine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Nefarian_Serpine "Nefarian Serpine") and [Baron Vengeous](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Baron_Vengeous "Baron Vengeous") and beat both into retreat. According to [Skulduggery Pleasant](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") she had a right hook that could have taken off someone's head and, according to legend, actually had once. According to Ghastly, she made a mistake by going against [Lord Vile](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Lord_Vile "Lord Vile") single-handedly, and was killed. Ghastly was also part of a group called [The Dead Men](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Dead_Men "The Dead Men").

After [The War](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_War "The War") ended, Ghastly didn't want to continue a career in magic, thus he opened his tailor shop.

[Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") and [Stephanie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") visit Ghastly in [his tailor shop](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Bespoke_Tailors "Bespoke Tailors"). He begins making protective clothing for [Stephanie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain"). [Stephanie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") tells Ghastly that they believe that [Serpine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Nefarian_Serpine "Nefarian Serpine") killed her uncle [Gordon](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Gordon_Edgley "Gordon Edgley") to get [the Sceptre of the Ancients](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sceptre_of_the_Ancients "The Sceptre of the Ancients"). Ghastly and [Stephanie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") get into an argument about how she is to young for the world of [magic](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic"). [Stephanie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") storms out, and [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") asks Ghastly if they can have access to his family’s collection of art and books about [the Ancients](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ancients "The Ancients") in [the Vault](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Vault "The Vault"). Ghastly does not give him permission and calls [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") irresponsible for allowing [Stephanie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") to be involved. [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") then leaves.

Two days later, when [Stephanie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain"), now having taken the name [Valkyrie Cain](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain"), shows up at [his tailorshop](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Bespoke_Tailors "Bespoke Tailors"), he tries to apologise for what he said, but she interrupts him, saying that [Serpine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Nefarian_Serpine "Nefarian Serpine") has abducted [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant"). He believes her and they drive to [the Waxworks Museum](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Waxworks_Museum "Waxworks Museum") to see [the Elders](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Irish_Council_of_Elders "The Irish Council of Elders"). [The Administrator](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Administrator_\(male\) "The Administrator (male)") insists they need an appointment to see them, and one of the [Cleavers](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Cleaver "Cleaver") blocks [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") when she tries to barge past. Ghastly argues and covinces [the Administrator](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Administrator_\(male\) "The Administrator (male)") to see if [Meritorious](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Eachan_Meritorious "Eachan Meritorious") would like to see them. [Meritorious](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Eachan_Meritorious "Eachan Meritorious") appears a while later. He is doubtful of [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") ’s claims, and says that if she is wrong, moving against [Serpine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Nefarian_Serpine "Nefarian Serpine") could mean war. [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") states the war has already begun. [Meritorious](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Eachan_Meritorious "Eachan Meritorious") relents and decides to help them.

A while later, they are ready to go rescue [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant"). [Meritorious](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Eachan_Meritorious "Eachan Meritorious") needs time to convince [Morwenna](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Morwenna_Crow "Morwenna Crow") and [Tome](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Sagacious_Tome "Sagacious Tome") that [Serpine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Nefarian_Serpine "Nefarian Serpine") has broken [the Truce](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Truce "The Truce"), and so is only sending two [Cleavers](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Cleaver "Cleaver") with them so as not to arrouse suspicion. **Ghastly** tells [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") that [Mr. Bliss](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Bliss "Mr. Bliss") has offered to help, sending someone to aid them. [Meritorious](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Eachan_Meritorious "Eachan Meritorious") leads them out if [the Museum](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Waxworks_Museum "Waxworks Museum") to a van where the two [Cleavers](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Cleaver "Cleaver") and [Tanith Low](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tanith_Low "Tanith Low"), sent by [Mr. Bliss](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Bliss "Mr. Bliss"), are waiting. They get in the van.

![Ghastlybespoke](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/skulduggery/images/5/5b/Ghastlybespoke.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20120526192141)

Arriving at [the castle](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Serpine%E2%80%99s_Castle "Serpine’s Castle"), the [Cleavers](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Cleaver "Cleaver") leap the fence in a jump and Ghasty gets over it by using air, landing the woodland surrounding [Serpine’s castle](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Serpine%E2%80%99s_Castle "Serpine’s Castle"). [Tanith](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tanith_Low "Tanith Low") boosts [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") over before following her herself. They approach [the castle](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Serpine%E2%80%99s_Castle "Serpine’s Castle"), and [Tanith](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tanith_Low "Tanith Low") sends the two [Cleavers](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Cleaver "Cleaver") to distract the mass of [Hollow Men](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Hollow_Men "Hollow Men") outside so they can get in, which Ghastly admonishes her for, stating she’s sent them to their death. [Tanith](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tanith_Low "Tanith Low") unlocks the door to the castle with her magic and they enter. They go to the basement, where they split up to check the various rooms. Ghastly and [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") meet up again and find [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant"), shackled to a chair in one room. Ghastly unshackles him, and he states he knows where [the key](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Gordon%E2%80%99s_Brooch "Gordon’s Brooch") is. They leave, running into a swarm of [Hollow Men](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Hollow_Men "Hollow Men"). [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") and Ghastly make a wall of fire to slow them and they jump out a window and run back to the van, passing one of the [Cleavers](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Cleaver "Cleaver"), now dead.

Later on, he joins Valkyrie, Skulduggery and Tanith to a warehouse to look for evidence on Serpine. [The White Cleaver](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_White_Cleaver "The White Cleaver") (which Serpine turned one of the dead Cleavers into) attacks them. Ghastly is forced to use the [Earth](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Elemental "Elemental") power to save himself by turning into a stone statue just as the scythe comes down.

### The Diablerie Returns

After spending two years as a statue, he finally returns to normal, and after being quickly checked by [Kenspeckle Grouse](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Kenspeckle_Grouse "Kenspeckle Grouse"), he is welcomed back by his friends. He aids Tanith in protecting [Fletcher Renn](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Fletcher_Renn "Fletcher Renn") at the [Hibernian Cinema](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Hibernian_Cinema "The Hibernian Cinema"). When the Hibernian is attacked by the [Diablerie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Diablerie "The Diablerie"), Ghastly engages in a fight with [Gruesome Krav](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Gruesome_Krav "Gruesome Krav"), but his fists do not affect him.

Afterwards, Ghastly tells Valkyrie about a vision his mother had about the world ending. Valkyrie dies and the world ends, but she doesn't care. She tells him that she is still staying, and Ghastly promises not to try to change her mind about getting her life back to normal.

Ghastly is also present at Liffey Bridge where the gang get [Guild](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Thurid_Guild "Thurid Guild") back, but lose Fletcher.

At [Aranmore Farm](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Aranmore_Farm "Aranmore Farm"), he tells Valkyrie that he has made new clothes for her. Ghastly is nearly killed by a Faceless One, but Skulduggery saves him just in time.

### The Revengers' Club

While helping Valkyrie track down [Skulduggery's skull](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant%27s_skull "Skulduggery Pleasant's skull"), [China](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/China_Sorrows "China Sorrows") makes Ghastly a façade. She burns two tattoos into him, and after twenty-four hours, he can transform his face into a normal one. He chooses to retain his face, and just remove the scars so that he no longer needed to use disguises. He is forced to stay out of the trade between the gang and [Thames Chabon](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Thames_Chabon "Thames Chabon"), the person believed to have the skull.

After the gang learn that [Davina Marr](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Davina_Marr "Davina Marr"), the new [Prime Detective](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Prime_Detective "Prime Detective"), had bought the real skull of Chabon. They plan to infilitrate the Sanctuary, but the plan goes wrong and they are all arrested. He is freed afterwards, but is arrested after he provides distraction from the Cleavers. Later on, he goes with Fletcher to check old pubs that [Sanguine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Billy-Ray_Sanguine "Billy-Ray Sanguine") used to frequent. He goes to Serpine's old castle to arrest [Scarab](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Dreylan_Scarab "Dreylan Scarab"), but the gang don't succeed, but they do rescue [Tanith](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tanith_Low "Tanith Low") and Grouse after they are kidnapped by [the Revengers' Club](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Revengers%27_Club "The Revengers' Club").

He also helps the gang stop the [Desolation Engine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Desolation_Engine "Desolation Engine") from going off at the [Croke Park Stadium](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Croke_Park_Stadium "Croke Park Stadium").

### Fighting Tesseract

He later claims that [Tesseract](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tesseract "Tesseract") was a powerful opponent.

### The Remnant Outbreak

Ghastly spends Christmas with Skulduggery and Ravel. When the Remnants are unleashed from the [Midnight Hotel](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Midnight_Hotel "The Midnight Hotel"), the Remnants possess Wreath, Shudder and [Corrival Deuce](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Corrival_Deuce "Corrival Deuce"), who all attack the three. Ghastly and Skulduggery escape when Ravel is possessed by another Remnant and Tesseract walks in. Ghastly and the others, including China flee to the Hibernian. They teleport to Gordon's estate to find information on how to trap all the Remnants in the [Receptacle](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Receptacle "The Receptacle"). They all see the Echo-Gordon, and Ghastly agrees to take Fletcher to [MacGillycuddy's Reeks](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/MacGillycuddy%27s_Reeks "MacGillycuddy's Reeks") in Kerry, where the Receptacle is, while Gordon teaches Fletcher how to teleport a large group of people. Skulduggery tells Ghastly to take the [Bentley](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/1954_Bentley_R-Type_Continental "1954 Bentley R-Type Continental") but must not destroy it.

When they returns to the Hibernian, Ghastly gathers the courage to ask Tanith on a date, to which she agrees and kisses him. He is possessed by a Remnant later on, and witnesses Darquesse. After the Remnants are captured, he attends the second meeting at the Great Chamber to elect another [Council of Elders](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Council_of_Elders "The Council of Elders"). When Ravel takes the job of Grand Mage, and [Madam Mist](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Madam_Mist "Madam Mist"), a [Children of the Spider](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Children_of_the_Spider "Children of the Spider"). takes one of the Elder positions, Skulduggery tells Ghastly that he should be Elder to find a way to get the Remannt that had possessed Tanith out of her. Despite objecting at first, he agrees.

### Duties as Elder

Due to his duties as an Elder Ghastly doesn't appear much. Skulduggery and Valkyrie visit the Sanctuary in [Roarhaven](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Roarhaven "Roarhaven") to report what they have to the Elders. Ghastly openly expresses he does not like being an Elder and is focusing all his powers on trying to find Remnant Tanith.

![Ghastly](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/skulduggery/images/5/55/Ghastly.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20170511083806)

When Skulduggery suggests they raid [The Temple](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Temple "The Temple") and kill [Melancholia St Clair](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Melancholia_St_Clair "Melancholia St Clair"), Ghastly stops him but agrees to put out an arrest warrant. Ghastly is then visited by Fletcher who had been dumped by Valkyrie and asks for advice. Ghastly helps Fletcher on how he could win Valkyrie back.

Ghastly is next seen at the [Requiem Ball](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Requiem_Ball "The Requiem Ball") with [Dexter Vex](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Dexter_Vex "Dexter Vex"), Skulduggery, Valkyrie, and China. Skulduggery, Valkyrie and Vex all tease Ghastly about his Elder status. As he is talking to [Eliza Scorn](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Eliza_Scorn "Eliza Scorn"), Melancholia sucks the life force out of him and all the others present at the ball. However, Valkyrie convinces her to give the souls back, and Ghastly is brought back to life with all the others.

### The Maleficent Seven

Ghastly has a disagreement with [Dexter Vex](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Dexter_Vex "Dexter Vex") about whether or not he should have been told about Dexter's mission. At this point he confesses that he want's to kill [Billy-Ray Sanguine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Billy-Ray_Sanguine "Billy-Ray Sanguine").

### The Betrayal

He was stabbed in the back by fellow Dead Man, Erskine Ravel, when he, Anton Shudder and Ravel returned to Roarhaven to confront Madame Mist about some of the new "security procedures" she put in place while they were away fighting against the Supreme Council.

Just before his death, Ghastly notices Ravel's golden tear filled eyes. Then Ravel sticks the blade into Ghastly's throat and leaves him to choke on his own blood.

### The Dying Of The Light

Ghastly appears as a ghost in the Necropolis during the second test. He appears to Skulduggery and talks to him alongside many other people from Skulduggery's past. Ghastly reveals that being a ghost, he is aware of Skulduggery's actions as Lord Vile but forgives them, he requests that Skulduggery join them in the afterlife, and later attempts to drag him down along with all of the other ghosts. However, Skulduggery escapes their grasp, telling them it is not time yet.

### Until the End

Ghastly's resurrection (brought about by [Darquesse](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Darquesse "Darquesse")) is revealed when Valkyrie visits Ghastly's office in the High Sanctuary after Darquesse's reconstruction of the universe.

## Powers and abilities

### Magic

Ghastly is an [Elemental](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Elemental "Elemental"). He once used the earth power in defense against the [White Cleaver](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/White_Cleaver "White Cleaver") Serpine had created, resulting in him being turned to stone for two years. Though he is gifted, he prefers to use his fists, which are very strong, as shown when Ghastly fights Tesseract with hand-to-hand combat.

### Other skills

Ghastly is a highly skilled boxer and tailor, skills which he inherited from his mother and father, respectively. He makes unique clothes that are able to withstand extreme damage e.g. Valkyrie's coat, which protects her from bullet impacts, cuts, and punches among other things. His boxing skills are mentioned several times.

## Personality

He can be very stubborn but is mostly good-natured. He is kind and caring. He demonstrates this stubbornness by refusing to show information concerning the [Sceptre of the Ancients](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sceptre_of_the_Ancients "The Sceptre of the Ancients"). Although he is gifted with the use of magic, he prefers fighting with his fists and would probably rather live a normal life without magic if he could.

## Relationships

### Tanith Low

Ghastly had a crush on [Tanith Low](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tanith_Low "Tanith Low") since he met her but was unable to work up the courage to tell her about his feelings. Ever since he reverted from a statue, subtle hints of romance have been sparked culminating in *[Mortal Coil](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mortal_Coil "Mortal Coil")* where he gets up the courage to ask her out. She agrees and kisses him without his façade. However she is possessed by a [Remnant](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Remnant "Remnant") at the end of the book. Since then Ghastly has accepted the position of Elder to find her and remove the remnant. According to Skulduggery, Ghastly, despite knowing that it is impossible to remove a remnant from a person after four days of possession, "he just doesn't want to believe it." Given that eleven months were spent tracking and finding Skulduggery's skull with the help of Tanith and Ghastly, it can be assumed that the two of them spent an awful lot of time together, this period of time being a nice way for the two to get to know each other more. Something to consider about their attraction to each other, is that Tanith's had a history with many partners; most of which being very attractive. Given that Ghastly has always been described as ugly, it should be taken into account that Tanith likes him for who he is (if you don't count that she "digs scars, too."), as opposed to liking someone just for appearance.

The Ghastly Bespoke character card from the Skulduggery Pleasant website.

Ghastly is described as being a tall and broad shouldered man with a strong boxer's physique, but the Ghastly in the parallel dimension that Valkyrie and later Skulduggery travels to had a physique that was more speed than strength. His face is riddged with symmetrical scars due to his mother being jinxed when she was pregnant, this probably being the reason for him being percieved as *ugly.*

## Trivia

- His favourite colour is green.
- He was friends with [Leonard Cohen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leonard_Cohen).
- When asked by a fan, Derek Landy confirmed that the additional scar to Ghastly's head in Cassandra Pharos' vision (in *Last Stand of Dead Men)* was inflicted by Billy-Ray Sanguine, as an act of jealousy that Tanith had chosen Ghastly.
- His height is 6'2(188 cm) feet.
