The **Spats Family** is a rich family that includes the relatives of [Carmelita Spats](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Carmelita_Spats "Carmelita Spats"). Most or all of them may be deceased.

## Members

- [Mr. Spats](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Spats "Mr. Spats") †
- [Mrs. Spats](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Mrs._Spats "Mrs. Spats") †
- [Bruce](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Bruce "Bruce")
- [Carmelita Spats](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Carmelita_Spats "Carmelita Spats")

Bruce is Carmelita's uncle, though it is unknown whether paternal or maternal.[^1]

Carmelita's parents do not seem very present, as they send her off to boarding school [^2] and then to the [Snow Scouts](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Snow_Scouts "Snow Scouts");[^1] however, they do send [Vice Principal Nero](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Vice_Principal_Nero "Vice Principal Nero") a concerned letter after *[The Daily Punctilio](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Daily_Punctilio "The Daily Punctilio")* warns them about dangerous books that [one of her teachers](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Kit_Snicket "Kit Snicket") is giving to the students.[^3]

When Carmelita is abducted ("adopted") by [Count Olaf](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Olaf "Count Olaf") and [Esmé Squalor](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Esm%C3%A9_Squalor "Esmé Squalor"), it is stated that they intend to burn her house down with her parents inside. It is presumable that they or the [Man with a Beard but No Hair](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Man_with_a_Beard_but_No_Hair "Man with a Beard but No Hair") and [Woman with Hair but No Beard](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Woman_with_Hair_but_No_Beard "Woman with Hair but No Beard") did such, and thus killed the Spats parents.[^1] It is unknown if Bruce survived the abduction by eagles; a man named Bruce is mentioned later at the [Hotel Denouement](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Hotel_Denouement "Hotel Denouement"), though it could potentially be someone else of the same name. If so, he still could have been killed by the [Hotel Denouement Fire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Hotel_Denouement_Fire "Hotel Denouement Fire").[^4]

It is also possible that Carmelita was killed in the [Hotel Denouement Fire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Hotel_Denouement_Fire "Hotel Denouement Fire"), though unconfirmed.

In the Netflix adaptation, Carmelita says that her parents let her do whatever she wanted. They are confirmed killed by Olaf and Esmé.[^5] Bruce does not appear in the series.[^6]
