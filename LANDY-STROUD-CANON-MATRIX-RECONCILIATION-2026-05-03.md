# Landy-Stroud Canon Matrix Reconciliation

This note reconciles `Landy-Stroud Matrix.md` with
`FANDOM-CANON-RULESYSTEMS-2026-05-03.md` without changing the registry schema.
The March matrix remains useful as vault doctrine, but the May 3 registry keeps
the source-canon axes narrower and checkable.

## Registry Crosswalk

| Axis | Stroud / Bartimaeus | Landy / Skulduggery Pleasant | Vault reading |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Identity | `true_name`, bynames, `essence` | name doctrine in `THREE-NAMES.md`; registry-backed control appears through `book_of_names` | Stroud is the stronger source for raw identity exposure. Landy is retained as vault doctrine for name shielding. |
| Manifestation | `form`, planes, summoned embodiment | `adept_discipline`, `necromancy`, object-held power | Stroud asks what form the instance takes. Landy asks what discipline or medium it operates through. |
| Binding | `summoning`, `dismissal`, `master_relation` | `sanctuary`, hidden society law, discipline consent/control | Stroud binds through true name and charge. Landy controls through institution, discipline, and lawful enforcement surfaces. |
| Failure mode | overlong service, weak charge, master/name exposure | Remnant possession, Sanctuary capture, unstable death energy | Stroud drift is loose binding. Landy drift is possession or institutional breach. |

## Reconciled Rule

Stroud is the binding ontology:

- identify the true name or protected byname
- describe the current form
- state the summoning charge
- define the dismissal or release condition

Landy is the discipline and institution layer:

- identify the operating discipline
- identify the governing Sanctuary or equivalent institution
- identify the object, name, or law surface that mediates control
- identify possession, breach, or unauthorized autonomy as the failure state

## Status

No registry update is required. The registry already carries the necessary
source-backed primitives:

- Bartimaeus: `true_name`, `summoning`, `dismissal`, `form`,
  `master_relation`, `essence`
- Skulduggery Pleasant: `sanctuary`, `secret_society`,
  `adept_discipline`, `necromancy`, `book_of_names`

The older `Landy-Stroud Matrix.md`, `SKULLDUGGERY - STROUD.md`, and
`THREE-NAMES.md` remain doctrine notes. This reconciliation only maps them onto
the active FANDOM canon rulesystem registry.
