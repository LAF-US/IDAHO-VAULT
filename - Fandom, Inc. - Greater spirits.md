**Greater spirits** (unofficial name. sometimes called greater entities) are [spirits](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Spirits "Spirits") of immense power greatly exceeding that of [marids](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Marid "Marid") and is then beyond classification.

They are rarely [summoned](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Summoning "Summoning") because they are destructive, intelligent, and difficult to contain.

They generally require multiple [magicians](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Magicians "Magicians") (it takes 2 to summon a marid. Bartimaeus estimated a minimum of 4 for [Ramuthra](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Ramuthra "Ramuthra")) and/or and an object of power, such as the [Ring of Solomon](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Ring_of_Solomon "Ring of Solomon") or the [Amulet of Samarkand](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Amulet_of_Samarkand "Amulet of Samarkand"), to be controlled.

It was considered unwise to summon them without some form of controlling object, as they could cause massive destruction when not contained. The presence of certain of these entities had the power to warp reality on a fundamental level, disrupting other existing magic and violating normal physical laws.

## Known Entities

- [Nouda](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Nouda "Nouda")
- [Ramuthra](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Ramuthra "Ramuthra")
- [Uraziel](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Uraziel "Uraziel"), the spirit of the Ring.
- The unnamed spirit trapped in the [Amulet of Samarkand](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Amulet_of_Samarkand).
