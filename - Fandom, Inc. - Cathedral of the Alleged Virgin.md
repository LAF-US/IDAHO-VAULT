The **Cathedral of the Alleged Virgin** was a cathedral near [the Hinterlands](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Hinterlands "The Hinterlands").

## Description

It was northeast of [Lousy Lane](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Lousy_Lane "Lousy Lane"), east of the [Grim River](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Grim_River "Grim River"), and west of the [Finite Forest](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Finite_Forest "Finite Forest").

It apparently has stained-glass windows.[^1]

## History

### The Hostile Hospital

When [Lemony Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Lemony_Snicket "Lemony Snicket") talks about "how music has charms to soothe a savage beast" (a quote by [William Congreve](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Congreve "wikipedia:William Congreve")), he mentions that he was ducking in the altar, typing, while his friend and associate hummed a tune that calmed him down and drowned out the noise of his typewriter.

### Lemony Snicket: The Unauthorized Autobiography

[Count Olaf](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Olaf "Count Olaf") 's associate, as she was searching for [Dr. Montgomery](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Montgomery_Montgomery "Montgomery Montgomery") 's reptiles, mentioned the cathedral. She had mistaken the cathedral for "a toad that looks like a church, complete with stained-glass eyes." [^2]

## Trivia

- The Cathedral is likely a Catholic Cathedral dedicated to [Mary, mother of Jesus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mary,_mother_of_Jesus "wikipedia:Mary, mother of Jesus"), a patron saint in the Catholic religion. Mary is believed by Catholics and Christians to be a virgin who miraculously gave birth to the son of God; the name of "Alleged Virgin" is a skeptical crack at this belief, along with beliefs for alleged "virgin" saints.
- On the map, footsteps are drawn near the cathedral.
- A sign pointing to the Cathedral of the Alleged Virgin can be seen in the [V.F.D. tunnels](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/V.F.D._tunnels "V.F.D. tunnels") in the Netflix adaptation in (at least) [A Bad Beginning: Part One](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/A_Bad_Beginning:_Pa***REMOVED***One "A Bad Beginning: Part One").
