Idaho Reports is a weekly public policy news show that airs Friday nights on Idaho Public Television. The longest-running legislative program in the west, Idaho Reports celebrates its 50th season this year rooted in IdahoPTV's history of public service and education for the people in Idaho. While staying true to our history of "statwide, in-depth, trusted" political reporting and analysis, Idaho Reports is also looking toward the future as we expand into more diversified digital spaces. 

The IR team has spent 2020 and 2021 working to expand our distribution beyond standard television broadcasts, striving to reach modern audiences where they look to find their news. Our current weekly lineup of Idaho Reports products includes regular print reporting on our blog, original podcast episodes on Wednesdays, email newsletters on Friday mornings, weekly coronavirus updates on Friday evenings, and the traditional half-hour broadcast show on Friday nights.

Newsletter: Beginning in the new fiscal year, July 2021, Idaho Reports puts out a weekly e-mail newsletter that is published Friday mornings with a roundup of our content from the week. As of early December, the IR newsletter has attracted 363 organic contacts and only 6 have ever unsubscribed. Across those contacts we have sent 5,590 emails with a 55% open rate, which is above the reported 24% industry average. Of those 2,994 opened emails, 75% were viewed on desktop and 25% were viewed on mobile. While desktop users make up a larger segment of newsletter readers who open the email, mobile users appear to engage with newsletter content at a slightly higher rate. Of 416 link clicks (examples: Read More, Listen, Watch), 61% came from desktop users and 39% came from mobile users.
