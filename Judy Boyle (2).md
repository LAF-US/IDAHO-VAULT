Judy Boyle is a 9th-term Republican Idaho House member from District 9B (Midvale, Washington County) who challenged Mike Moyle for Majority Leader in November 2020 and is one of the six most senior House Republicans as of the 2023 session.

## Basic Info

- **District:** 9B (Midvale, Washington County — southwest Idaho)
- **Chamber:** House
- **Terms:** 9th (in office since 2009)
- **Occupation:** Agriculture and writer; seven-generation farm family
- **Education:** Lassen College; Boise State University; University of Idaho
- **Party:** Republican (100% ACU rating; NRA "Defender of Freedom" award)
- **Email:** JBoyle@house.idaho.gov
- **Statehouse phone:** (208) 332-1064

## Background

Worked as natural resources director for Congresswoman Helen Chenoweth; lobbyist for Idaho Farm Bureau. Chair, Western Legislative Forestry Task Force. Born Midvale, Idaho.

## Current Committees (2026)

Resources & Conservation, State Affairs, Transportation & Defense. Not on JFAC or any appropriations committee.

## Political Significance

Listed in Logan Finney's November 2022 power vacuum analysis as one of the six most senior House Republicans entering 2023. **Challenged Mike Moyle for Majority Leader in November 2020** — lost but demonstrates willingness to dissent from leadership on structural questions. No current stake in defending the JFAC working group structure.

## Interview Value

Floor member who votes on JFAC budget bills without access to working group deliberations. Best question: "As a floor member, how do you find out what decisions were made in the working groups before the public vote — is that information available to you?" Not a JFAC insider; cannot speak to mechanics, but can speak to downstream information asymmetry. Her 2020 leadership challenge is evidence of independence from Moyle on structural questions.
