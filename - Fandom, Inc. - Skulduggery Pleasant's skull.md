Skulduggery's Skull

**Skulduggery Pleasant's skull**, also known as **the Murder Skull**, was a key item in the [fourth book](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant:_Dark_Days "Skulduggery Pleasant: Dark Days"), *Dark Days*. [Valkyrie Cain](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") used it as the [Isthmus Anchor](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Isthmus_Anchor "Isthmus Anchor") to get Skulduggery Pleasant back from [the Faceless Ones' universe](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Faceless_Ones%27_universe "The Faceless Ones' universe").

## History

### Theft

Skulduggery had his head stolen by [Goblins](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Goblin "Goblin"). Around the year 1987, Skulduggery was hired to investigate a church plagued by "angry spirits." He found out that Goblins infested the church. Skulduggery set up camp, waiting for them to arrive at the church. The first night he leapt out screaming and throwing fireballs, hoping to scare them away permanently. The second night, he crept up behind them and repeated his actions. However, on the third night, the Goblins approached Skulldugery's camp instead and stole his head while he was "meditating." Skulduggery did not realize this until the morning.

He acquired another skull by means of a poker game.

### Events that Followed

Skulduggery said that the Goblins ran into a sorcerer named [Larks](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Larks "Larks"), who took the skull from them. It was then bought by a women as a wedding gift for her husband. She then used the skull to kill her husband after figuring out he was stealing her assets. The murder was picked up by mortal police, and the skull was given the nicknamed the "Murder Skull." The skull found its way to the black market and changed hands four times. A sorcerer named [Umbra](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Umbra "Umbra"), sensing the [magic](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") inside, claimed the skull. Afterward, a man called [Thames Chabon](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Thames_Chabon "Thames Chabon") acquired it.

Valkyrie, hoping to use Skulduggery's skull to save him from the [Faceless Ones](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Faceless_Ones "Faceless Ones"), managed to arrange a meeting with Thames through the [v](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire "Vampire") [ampire](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire "Vampire"), [Caelan](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Caelan "Caelan"). However, Chabon sells her a fake, selling the real skull for triple the price to [Davina Marr](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Davina_Marr "Davina Marr").

![Skulddugerypleasantmskul](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulddugerypleasantmskul.jpg)

### In the Sanctuary

Davina Marr, the new [Prime Detective](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Prime_Detective "Prime Detective"), bought the skull to keep the portal between dimensions from reopening. Valkyrie, along with [Fletcher Renn](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Fletcher_Renn "Fletcher Renn"), [Tanith Low](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tanith_Low "Tanith Low"), and [Ghastly Bespoke](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Ghastly_Bespoke "Ghastly Bespoke"), try to steal it from the [Repository](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Repository "Repository"), but get captured in the process. Valkyrie managed to steal it with Fletcher, but leaves Tanith and Ghastly behind. She, Fletcher, and [China Sorrows](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/China_Sorrows "China Sorrows") go to [Aranmore Farm](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Aranmore_Farm "Aranmore Farm"), where they are able to reopen the portal and bring Skulduggery back from the realm of the Faceless Ones.

## Current Situation

Skulduggery now wears his original skull. He claims the one he won from the poker game is now a "spare." Twice it has been mentioned that his skull's cheekbones are higher than that of his former skull.
