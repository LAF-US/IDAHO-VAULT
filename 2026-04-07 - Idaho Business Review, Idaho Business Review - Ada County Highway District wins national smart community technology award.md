ACHD received the Smart Community Award at CentralSquare’s annual ENGAGE conference, held March 15-18 in Washington, D.C. The award recognizes the district’s work transforming how it communicates, plans and delivers development services across the.

Guided by its “Build Together” initiative, ACHD implemented CentralSquare COMMDEV and Laserfiche software to automate permitting and impact fee processes. The move reduced errors, eliminated incomplete submissions, and helped protect critical infrastructure funding while improving overall service delivery.

More than 1,400 government and public safety leaders from across the United States attended the conference, which focused on practical strategies including cloud technology, workflow automation, artificial intelligence and workforce challenges.

“We’re proud to celebrate the heroes who protect, serve, and strengthen their communities across the country,” said Manolis Kotzabasakis, CEO of CentralSquare. “These organizations and individuals exemplify the dedication, innovation, and leadership that move the public sector forward and make a meaningful difference every day.”
