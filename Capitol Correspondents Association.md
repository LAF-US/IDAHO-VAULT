The Capitol Correspondents Association (CCA) is the press credentialing body for the Idaho Legislature whose president Kevin Richert co-signed the March 2026 open-meetings letter to JFAC.

## Structure

- President: Kevin Richert (Idaho Education News)
- Standing committee member: Logan Finney (Idaho Reports)
- Approximately a dozen members credentialed to cover the Idaho Legislature

## 2026 JFAC Challenge

The CCA sent a formal open-meetings complaint letter March 4, 2026 to JFAC co-chairs Scott Grow and Josh Tanner. Logan Finney drafted the CCA briefing document and member email coordinating the ~dozen CCA members ahead of the letter.
