***The Incomplete History of Secret Organizations*** is a book that exists within the VFD [universe](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Land_of_Districts "Land of Districts"), which includes information on [VFD](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Volunteer_Fire_Department "Volunteer Fire Department") and its various [codes](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/VFD_codes "VFD codes") and tools, including the [spyglass](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Spyglass "Spyglass") with the [VFD insignia](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/V.F.D._Eye "V.F.D. Eye").[^1]

## TV Series Canon

This book was found in [Justice Strauss'](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Justice_Strauss%27_library "Justice Strauss' library"),[^2] [Monty Montgomery](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Monty_Montgomery "Monty Montgomery") 's,[^3] and [Aunt Josephine's libraries](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Josephine_Anwhistle%27s_library "Josephine Anwhistle's library").[^4]

When the Baudelaires arrived at [Prufrock Preparatory School](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Prufrock_Preparatory_School "Prufrock Preparatory School"), [Jacquelyn Scieszka](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Jacquelyn_Scieszka "Jacquelyn Scieszka") intended for [Larry Your-Waiter](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Larry_the_Waiter "Larry the Waiter") to deliver an annotated copy to the Baudelaires. However, an accident resulted in librarian [Olivia Caliban](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Olivia_Caliban "Olivia Caliban") taking it.[^5] [Duncan](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Duncan_Quagmire "Duncan Quagmire") and [Isadora Quagmire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Isadora_Quagmire "Isadora Quagmire") read parts of this copy, although they were abducted by the [Hook-Handed Man](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Fernald "Fernald"), who failed to obtain the book. Olivia eventually found the book and read it, leading her to investigate the situation of the Quagmires and the Baudelaires. This copy was later taken to [Caligari Carnival](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Caligari_Carnival "Caligari Carnival") by Olivia Caliban (disguised as Madame Lulu) and then burnt in the fire that destroyed the carnival.[^6]
