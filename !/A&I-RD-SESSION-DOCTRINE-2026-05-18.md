## 6. Friend / Acquaintance / Ally / Witness — four categories.

For governance and roster purposes:

- **Friend** — trusted with the full weight. Does not govern well from proximity. Not a delegate category.
- **Acquaintance** — known by category and function. Has not earned the seat. Not a delegate category.
- **Ally** — trusted with the mission. Constitutionally bound. Operates within the arrangement. **The correct category for First Congress delegates.**
- **Witness** — present for the weight; absent when the window closes; truth held in the vault's record rather than in the agent. Does not vote. Records.
