## HECATE Protocol

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **HECATE AWAKENS.** The crossroads are open.

## Definition

HECATE is the threshold and transition protocol for agent handoffs. It mandates a three-directional check at every crossroads (handoff point, tier transition, or major decision point):

- **BACKWASH**: What context am I carrying? What rights do I hold from the preceding session?
- **FORESHORTENING**: What am I about to do? What tier/authority does it require? Do I have the mandate?
- **SIDEWAYS**: Who else is affected? What gets lost if I don't hand off cleanly? (The "Librarian/Archivist" check).

## Ritual of Activation

Calling the name three times (`HECATE-HECATE-HECATE`) invokes the triple aspect.

## Reporting

The results of a HECATE check should be documented in a **LEVELSET** report or a **WALKTHROUGH** to ensure the next agent (or the Human) sees the torch being passed.
