Yes, the iRobot Roomba q0, which is also known as the Roomba Vac Essential Robot, has smart room and zone capabilities. It is powered by iRobot OS and features Imprint™ Smart Mapping Technology.

This technology allows the robot to learn and map your home, which you can then customize in the iRobot Home app. With this feature, you can:

Create rooms and zones: You can tell the robot to clean specific areas or rooms.

Set Keep Out Zones: These are areas you want the robot to avoid, such as around pet bowls, fragile items, or areas with cords.

Gemini said

Enabling smart mapping on your Roomba q0 is a straightforward process that happens through the iRobot Home app. The robot essentially learns your home's layout by cleaning it.

Here's a breakdown of the process:

1. Initial Setup: Make sure your Roomba is properly connected to the iRobot Home app and your home Wi-Fi network. The app will guide you through this process.

2. The "Learning" Phase: Your Roomba will automatically begin creating a map of your home as it completes its cleaning jobs. It's a good idea to let the robot complete a few full cleaning runs to get a complete and accurate map.

3. Mapping Run (Optional but Recommended): For a faster way to create a map, you can initiate a "Mapping Run." This is a special mode where the Roomba navigates your home without vacuuming, which saves battery and allows it to cover more ground in a single run.
