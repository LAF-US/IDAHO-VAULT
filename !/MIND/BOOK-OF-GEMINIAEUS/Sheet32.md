4. Transparent "Why" Logic

Folks are often suspicious of AI because it feels like a "black box."

"Show Your Work": Every time M365 suggests a change or a formula, a small "Why?" link is available. Clicking it shows the specific data points it used (e.g., "Based on your 'Project X' folder structure"). This removes the "sneaky" feeling by proving it's using his own files, not global trends.
