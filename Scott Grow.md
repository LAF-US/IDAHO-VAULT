Scott Grow is the Republican Idaho Senate member from District 14 (Eagle) serving as JFAC Senate co-chair since 2023 and co-recipient of the CCA open-meetings letter in March 2026.

## Basic Info

- **District:** 14 (Eagle, western Ada County)
- **Chamber:** Senate
- **Role:** JFAC Senate co-chair (2023–present)
- **Party:** Republican
- **Background:** Former educator

## JFAC Role

Selected by Mike Moyle and Senate leadership following Jeff Agenbroad's retirement. Eagle district overlaps with Josh Tanner's District 22A — both co-chairs from a 15-mile radius of western Ada County.

Has publicly stated that JFAC is to his knowledge the only joint appropriations committee of its kind in the 50 states.

Co-signed the February 2023 letter (with Wendy Horman) formalizing working group structure without a committee vote — an improper instrument under JFAC Rule 11.

## Open Meetings Challenge

Co-recipient of CCA letter, March 4, 2026. Had not publicly responded to open-meetings questions as of March 12, 2026. Statutory response deadline: ~March 18, 2026.
