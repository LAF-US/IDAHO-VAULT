The Blue Djinn of Babylon is the symbolic leader of all djinn. The office sits above the ordinary good/evil split and is framed as a position of reason, logic, and judgment rather than simple power.

The page is useful because it makes the series' political metaphysics explicit:

- the Blue Djinn is feminine
- the office is above ordinary faction
- the position is tied to rules and penalties
- succession is part of the structure, not an accident

That makes the Blue Djinn a good institutional analogue for a vault authority surface: a role that arbitrates rather than merely commands.
