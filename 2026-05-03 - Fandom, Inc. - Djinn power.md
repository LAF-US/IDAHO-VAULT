Djinn power is explicitly tied to lifeforce and limits.

The page emphasizes that every wish costs the djinn something real, usually lifespan or usable force. Younger djinn need more heat, adults can do more, and the power has clear boundaries: no reviving the dead, no controlling time, no copying oneself, and no undoing another djinn's spells except in the special case of the Blue Djinn of Babylon.

That makes this page important for the vault because it shows the difference between capability and entitlement. A being can be powerful and still be bounded by law, heat, age, and cost.
