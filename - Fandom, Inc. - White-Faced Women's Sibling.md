The **Third Sibling of the White-Faced Women** was a sibling of the two [white-faced women](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/White-Faced_Women "White-Faced Women") who were [associates](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Olaf%27s_Associates "Count Olaf's Associates") of [Count Olaf](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Olaf "Count Olaf").

## Biography

*Note: This includes information that is from both the books (canon) and the 2017-2019 Netflix Adaptation (which has its own canon). They will be referenced accordingly.*

The Sibling was born, with their sisters, as the "world's first" conjoined triplets in [Heimlich Hospital](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Heimlich_Hospital "Heimlich Hospital"), attached at the hip. They were described as completely identical. Later, they were successfully separated from their sisters, presumably by surgery. ￼ [^1]

At some point, their home burnt to the ground. They perished in the fire, while their sisters escaped.[^2] At the time, the Official Fire Department declared it an accident.[^1] However, later, the sisters speculated that their home was destroyed by arson, possibly by [Count Olaf](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Olaf "Count Olaf") or another member of [VFD](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Volunteer_Fire_Department "Volunteer Fire Department").

## Trivia

- While in the [Netflix](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Netflix "Netflix") [adaptation](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/A_Series_of_Unfortunate_Events_\(TV_series\) "A Series of Unfortunate Events (TV series)"), they are referred to as female,[^3] and noted to be identical to their sisters,[^1] in the book series they are only referred to as a "sibling."
- Those who seem to be twins really being triplets is a recurring motif in the series. Just like the [Quagmires](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Quagmire_Family "Quagmire Family") and [Denouements](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Denouement_Family "Denouement Family"), the [White-Faced Women](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/White-Faced_Women "White-Faced Women") appear to be twins before the third sibling is revealed in [The Slippery Slope](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Slippery_Slope "The Slippery Slope").
