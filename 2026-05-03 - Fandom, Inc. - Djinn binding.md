Djinn binding is the rule-enforcement layer of the series.

The page lists bindings that act as consequences or controls, including Methuselah Binding, Memory Binding, and Firefoot Binding. These are not cosmetic effects; they are structural constraints that change age, memory, or bodily outcome.

For the vault, this is the useful distinction: the world does not rely on vague punishment. It uses explicit bindings with predictable outcomes.
