# Book of Codices - Runtime Shard Discovery

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Historical architectural dialogue. Preserve this page as thought-history, not
> as a literal operating procedure. Current live CrewAI doctrine and topology
> live in `.crewai/MANIFEST.md`.

This page records the architectural turn where the vault stopped being imagined
as a laptop-bound container and became legible as a layered system:

- `CANON` = durable authority
- `DRIVE` = active workspace
- `RUNTIME` = disposable execution slice
- `ARCHIVE` = cold preserved memory

The discovery mattered because it made one boundary explicit:

**the laptop is not the vault**

## Durable Doctrines Extracted

- Portable authority matters more than local romance.
- The vault should be remote-first, with a local runtime slice.
- `CANON`, `DRIVE`, `RUNTIME`, and `ARCHIVE` must stay distinct.
- Promotion rules matter more than storage format.
- Historical reasoning can illuminate doctrine without becoming doctrine.

## Historical Particulars That Are Not Binding

This discovery does not rewrite canon by itself. It illuminates the shape of
