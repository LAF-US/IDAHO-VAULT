Top of the ticket races get the most coverage in any given election cycle, but those elected in down-ticket legislative and local races also make critical decisions that affect the everyday lives of Idahoans. Dr. Jaclyn Kettler of Boise State University’s BSU School of Public Service joins Melissa Davlin this week to discuss some of this year’s hot legislative face-offs.
