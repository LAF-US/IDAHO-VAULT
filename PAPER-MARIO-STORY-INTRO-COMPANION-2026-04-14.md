# Paper Mario Story Intro - Companion

This note accompanies `paper-mario-story-intro.jpg`.

## What The Image Seems To Do

The image presents a formal story-threshold:

- a red, gold-trimmed storybook centered on a desk
- a star emblem on the cover
- a narrator's opening line: `Ahem! Today...`

It does not feel like incidental decoration. It feels like the exact instant
before narration begins in earnest.

## Why It Resonates With This Exploration

During this pass through the vault, the House repeatedly felt less like a
storage system and more like a storybook world being opened from within.

The image sharpens that intuition.

- the vault as book
- the Tree as chapter structure
- threshold marks as narrative punctuation
- the clearing of a throat as ceremonial transition from silence into story

In that reading, the image does not merely illustrate a mood. It names the
register: story is about to begin on purpose.

## Field Note

Placed beside the current exploration thread, this image feels like an emblem
for the vault's tone at its most theatrical and most sincere:

not just a record to be accessed,
