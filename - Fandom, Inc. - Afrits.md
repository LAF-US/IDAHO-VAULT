**Afrits** are the second most powerful classified form of spirits. They are mentioned to be [demons](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Demons "Demons") of fire. Afrits are usually employed by high officials in the government.

As with the different spirit classes, power among afrits differ and can be divided further to levels.

## Known Afrits

- [Honorius](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Honorius "Honorius") - a ninth-level afrit of [William Gladstone](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/William_Gladstone "William Gladstone").
- [Naeryan](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Naeryan "Naeryan") - a female afrit who took part in the Spirit Uprising.
- [Shubit](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Shubit "Shubit") - [Jessica Whitwell](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Jessica_Whitwell "Jessica Whitwell") 's polite afrit.

## Other Afrits Mentioned

- Mormel
- Patterknife
- Phoebus
- Tchue - a spirit with reputation and seems to be a master of words (described by [Bartimaeus](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Bartimaeus "Bartimaeus") in various footnotes). Bartimaeus includes defeating Tchue in one of his boasts.
- Unnamed dark-green afrit of [Rupert Devereaux](https://bartimaeus.fandom.com/wiki/Rupert_Devereaux "Rupert Devereaux").

## Trivia

- In some cultures, the chief Affrit/Ihfrit is named Iblis/Eblis. Such spirits are a type of (d)jinn.
