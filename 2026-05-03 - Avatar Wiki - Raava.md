Raava is the spirit of light and peace. After permanently fusing with Wan,
she becomes the Avatar Spirit. She is the continuity carrier that enables the
Avatar Cycle, the Avatar State, and the transfer of past-life knowledge.

Raava can be harmed or destroyed, and the continuity with past lives can be
lost when she is separated from the Avatar.

## Vault Use

Raava is the continuity engine for reincarnation, memory, and Avatar State
authority.
