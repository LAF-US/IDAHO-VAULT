[[Keith Bybee]] is the [[Legislative Services Office|LSO]] Staff Director / Budget and Policy Analysis who staffs both [[Joint Finance-Appropriations Committee|JFAC]] and [[Executive Office of Revenue and Administration Committee|EORAC]], was CC'd on the [[Capitol Correspondents Association CCA Letter March 2026|March 2026 CCA letter]], and in March 2024 unilaterally changed intent language in a budget bill after JFAC voted.

## Basic Info

- **Role:** LSO Staff Director, Budget and Policy Analysis
- **Email:** JBybee@lso.idaho.gov
- **Committees staffed:** [[Joint Finance-Appropriations Committee|JFAC]] and [[Executive Office of Revenue and Administration Committee|EORAC]]

## Bybee Incident (March 2024)

Unilaterally changed intent language in the Chinden Campus budget bill after JFAC voted, without co-chairs' knowledge. House passed the changed version. Bybee invoked [[JFAC Rules|JFAC Rule 9(b)(ii)]] ministerial-correction authority. [[Wendy Horman]] said she was unaware and would have brought any change back to the full committee. Reported by [[Clark Corbin]], [[Idaho Capital Sun]], March 28, 2024.
