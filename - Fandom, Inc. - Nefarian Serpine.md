**Nefarian Serpine** was one of [Mevolent](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent "Mevolent") 's [Three Generals](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Three_Generals "The Three Generals") during [the War](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_War "The War").

## Biography

### History

Nefarian Serpine was Mevolent's most trusted General during the great war. He masterminded the death of [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") 's wife and child, killing them in front of him, knowing that Skulduggery would get too angry to think properly. He was right - Skulduggery, in his rage, wanted to kill Serpine without using his magic and grabbed the nearest weapon, which was a poisoned dagger which Serpine had laid out for him. Serpine then captured, tortured and finally killed him. Skulduggery returned seeking revenge. However, before he could punish Serpine [the Truce](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Truce "The Truce") stopped him.

Although Mevolent is dead, Serpine still plotted the return of the [Faceless Ones](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Faceless_Ones "Faceless Ones"). He spent several years pooling resources while Skulduggery tried to prove to the Elders that Serpine was still evil, but the Elders refused to believe that he would break the Truce.

During [the war](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_War "The War"), Serpine fought [Mr. Bliss](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Bliss "Mr. Bliss") in an unknown location while a hurricane destroyed everything around them. After this fight they didn’t meet again until the events of *[Skulduggery Pleasant](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant_\(book\) "Skulduggery Pleasant (book)").*

In 1861 The Dead Men chased him in the wild west but escaped with the help of the Necromancer [Noche](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Noche "Noche"). He also fought at [The Battle of Black Rock](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Black_Rock "The Battle of Black Rock") and was driven into retreat by [Ghastly Bespoke's mother](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Ghastly_Bespoke%27s_mother "Ghastly Bespoke's mother").

When [Mevolent](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent "Mevolent") realises he is losing [the war](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_War "The War"), he orders a last strike against the leaders of his opposition. Because of this, Nefarian Serpine kills [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") ’s wife and child before his eyes. [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant"), in his rage, wanted to physically kill Serpine, and so instinctively reached for a dagger which Serpine had coated in poison. Serpine captures and tortures [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") for a few days before killing him with [his red right hand](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Serpine%E2%80%99s_Red_Right_Hand "Serpine’s Red Right Hand") on the 23rd of October. His body is burned on a pike alongside those of the others leaders killed, causing [Mevolent](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent "Mevolent") to gain the upper hand in [the war](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_War "The War").

Serpine kills [Gordon Edgley](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Gordon_Edgley "Gordon Edgley") for not giving him [the key](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Gordon%E2%80%99s_Brooch "Gordon’s Brooch") to [the Caves](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Caves_of_the_Void "Caves of the Void"), where he has discovered [the Sceptre of the Ancients](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sceptre_of_the_Ancients "The Sceptre of the Ancients") is located. He then hires [Vindick Leather](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Vindick_Leather "Vindick Leather") to find [the key](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Gordon%E2%80%99s_Brooch "Gordon’s Brooch"). [Vindick](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Vindick_Leather "Vindick Leather") fails and dies at the hands of [Stephanie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain"), however, after she pulls him into a river in Dublin City, and the water tears him apart.

Serpine has a meeting with [Mr. Bliss](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Bliss "Mr. Bliss") who has been sent by [the Elders](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Council_of_Elders "The Council of Elders"), in [his castle](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Serpine%E2%80%99s_Castle "Serpine’s Castle") full of [Hollow Men](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Hollow_Men "Hollow Men"). During the meeting [Mr. Bliss](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Bliss "Mr. Bliss") informs Serpine that two Sancuary agents, [Clement Gale](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Clement_Gale "Clement Gale") and [Alexander Slake](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Alexander_Slake "Alexander Slake"), who had been monitoring him were found dead yesterday. Serpine lies, denying having anything to do with it, and [Mr. Bliss](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tanith_Low "Tanith Low") leaves to catch and plane to [London](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/London "London").

When [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") and [Stephanie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") go to [Grimwood House](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Gordon_Edgley%27s_house "Gordon Edgley's house") to look for the entrance to [the Caves](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Caves_of_the_Void "Caves of the Void"), Serpine arrives with four [Hollow Men](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Hollow_Men "Hollow Men"). **Serpine** incapacitates [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") and sends a [Hollow Man](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Hollow_Men "Hollow Men") after [Stephanie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain"). She defeats the Hollow Man by throwing them out of a window, but by then Serpine has left with [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant").

That night, in [his castle](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Serpine%E2%80%99s_Castle "Serpine’s Castle"), Serpine descends down, arriving at a door to a room where [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") is bound, tied to a chair. Serpine talks to him about how [the Elders](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Irish_Council_of_Elders "The Irish Council of Elders") are too nervous to come for him yet, and asks [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") where [the key](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Gordon%E2%80%99s_Brooch "Gordon’s Brooch") to [the Caves of the Void](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Caves_of_the_Void "Caves of the Void") is. When [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") feigns ignorance, Serpine shows him a new torture method he has picked up since killing [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant"). He makes the wood in a chair in the room expand and contract until it splinters, and then begins to do that to [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant").

[Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") is rescued that night, by during the rescue Serpine’s [Hollow Men](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Hollow_Men "Hollow Men") catch [a Cleaver](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Black_Cleaver "The Black Cleaver") and bring it to him. Serpine abandons [the castle](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Serpine%E2%80%99s_Castle "Serpine’s Castle"), bringing [the Cleaver](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Black_Cleaver "The Black Cleaver") with him.

In a warehouse that has been cleared out, Serpine revives [the Cleaver](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Black_Cleaver?so=search) using “a mixture of science and sorcery,” seeming to pump shadows into [the Cleaver](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Black_Cleaver?so=search) ’s body.

After [Mr. Bliss](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Bliss "Mr. Bliss") retrieves [the key](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Gordon%E2%80%99s_Brooch "Gordon’s Brooch") to [the Caves](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Caves_of_the_Void "Caves of the Void") and gives it to Serpine, he uses it to open the entrance to [the Caves](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Caves_of_the_Void "Caves of the Void") and enters with a group of [Hollow Men](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Hollow_Men "Hollow Men") to find [the Sceptre](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sceptre_of_the_Ancients "The Sceptre of the Ancients"). He finds it in a wooden chest. When he picks it up, it starts to ring and [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") appears. The fight, and [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") sets him on fire and beats him up, but his injuries heal in seconds. He uses a tendril of purple vapor to retrieve the fallen [Sceptre](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sceptre_of_the_Ancients "The Sceptre of the Ancients"). He tries to uses [the Sceptre](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sceptre_of_the_Ancients "The Sceptre of the Ancients") to destroy [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant"), but [Stephanie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain"), having now taken the name [Valkyrie Cain](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain"), rams into him, causing him to miss. [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") and [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") flee, but [Serpine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Serpine%E2%80%99s_Castle "Serpine’s Castle") and his [Hollow Men](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Hollow_Men "Hollow Men") chase them. He follows them outside [Grimwood House](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Grimwood_House "Grimwood House"), where, with the aid of [Tanith Low](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tanith_Low "Tanith Low") they’re holding off his [Hollow Men](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Hollow_Men "Hollow Men"). A car pulls up and they jump in as it speeds off.

Using the Sceptre he kills two of the Elders, [Eachan Meritorious](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Eachan_Meritorious "Eachan Meritorious") and [Morwenna Crow](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Morwenna_Crow "Morwenna Crow"), and the third Elder, [Sagacious Tome](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Sagacious_Tome "Sagacious Tome"), is revealed to be another traitor. However, when Serpine still can't access the [Book of Names](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Book_of_Names "Book of Names"), he kills the last Elder.

After Skulduggery and Valkyrie realise that Serpine's true plan is to use the Book of Names to control someone who knows how to bring the [Faceless Ones](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Faceless_Ones "Faceless Ones") back, the two try to stop him at [the Sanctuary](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Irish_Sanctuary "The Irish Sanctuary"). During the battle, Mr. Bliss is revealed to actually be against Serpine. Serpine also destroys the Book of Names by accident using the Sceptre. His plan failed, Serpine decides to torture Valkyrie using his red hand, a Necromancy trick. However, Skulduggery uses the sceptre and fires it on Serpine, killing him.

### Kingdom of the Wicked

In an alternate reality known as the [Leibniz Universe](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Leibniz_Universe "Leibniz Universe"), Serpine is still alive, and has betrayed [Mevolent](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent "Mevolent"). However, he still worships The Faceless Ones. He attempted to join [China Sorrows](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/China_Sorrows "China Sorrows") ' Resistance. China, however, does not trust him completely and has him wear a glove that suppresses his magical abilities. He serves as a guide for Skulduggery and Valkyrie into Mevolent's Palace, and later convinces them to remove his glove, fighting [Baron Vengeous](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Baron_Vengeous "Baron Vengeous") alongside Skulduggery. However, he betrays them and turns the Sceptre over to the alternate universe's China Sorrows. Later, after [Darquesse](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Darquesse "Darquesse") is transported to the alternate universe, Serpine murders China with his red hand from behind, and attempts to take the Sceptre for himself. He fails, and the Sceptre is transported along with Darquesse.

### The Dying of the Light

When Valkyrie is taken prisoner in the Alternate Dimension, Serpine rescues her. He is then revealed to be the new leader of [The Resistance](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Resistance "The Resistance"), following China’s death. He offers Valkyrie a deal that if she confronts Mevolent and pretends she is [Darquesse](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Darquesse "Darquesse") he will free Skulduggery and [Erskine Ravel](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Erskine_Ravel "Erskine Ravel"), she agrees. She confronts Mevolent and he shoots her with the [Magic-Sucker](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Magic-Sucker "Magic-Sucker"). Since Valkyrie’ Magic had been taken away, it does nothing, and Mevolent discards it. Peregrine then teleports behind him and takes it. As Mevolent approaches Valkyrie he returns and teleports them both away. When she is back in the village, Serpine takes her to Skulduggery and Ravel. When the Magic-Sucker is stolen by Skulduggery and Valkyrie, Serpine had already predicted this, and he confronts them with many sorcerers and orders them to hand it over. Vile is noticed by Valkyrie and he destroys the Magic-Sucker. Serpine is then teleported away by Peregrine.

## Personality

A psychopathic individual, Serpine was regarded by many to be insane, Skulduggery included. In many ways he was a fanatical terrorist with a religious agenda who believed that all human life except his own was insignificant and seeked to destroy humanity in the name of his gods. Nefarian presented himself in a cultured and sophisticated manner and possessed a dry, cynical sense of humour and a eloquent, theatrical way of speaking. Serpine was highly intelligent and cunning, capable of creating complex and unpredictable plans to achieve his goals. He was also sadistic, relishing in torturing people - even going so far as to target their loved ones, as he did with Skulduggery Pleasant. Despite being very calm and reserved in the best of times, Serpine was capable of frightening outbursts of rage when his plans go wrong, which makes him dangerous but very reckless.

## Magic and abilities

### Magic

Serpine is an [Adept](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Adept "Adept"). His magical discipline is [Energy-Throwing](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Energy-Throwing "Energy-Throwing"), and his energy streams are powerful enough to resist a shadow blast from [Lord Vile](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Lord_Vile "Lord Vile") for a short period of time. Blasts from Serpine's left hand are purple, their 'natural' colour, while blasts from his right hand are red.

Serpine is best known for his skinless red right hand, however, which uses a [Necromantic](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Necromancy "Necromancy") technique to kill any living or undead creature through agonising pain, simply by pointing at them. Visually, this takes the appearance of red vapour. The only known survivor of this is [Skulduggery Pleasant](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant"), and this is only due to [Auron Tenebrae](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Auron_Tenebrae "Auron Tenebrae") influencing Serpine's magic to guarantee Skulduggery's survival. The Serpine of the [Leibniz Universe](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Leibniz_Universe "Leibniz Universe") refines this technique further, so that it also strips the flesh from the part of the victim that he points at. The red right hand is limited, however, in that its victims must be within range.

Serpine is also a collector of magic, which he is able to accumulate in his red right hand through unknown means. By [2007](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant_\(book\) "Skulduggery Pleasant (book)"), Serpine is capable of neutralising bullets with a wave, creating and manipulating tendrils of purple vapour, causing metal to rust with a touch, causing objects to expand and contract, and manipulating hand-like projections from walls. The Leibniz Universe version of Serpine never learns these abilities, however.

### Abilities

Serpine also has quick reflexes, able to catch a dagger that Mr Bliss threw at him. It can be presumed that he has some skill in knife combat, often carrying a dagger around with him. He is skilled, but not that strong in melee combat. This is shown on his Battle card which give him a six for fighting ability.

## Description

Neferian is a tall man (although not as big as [Mr Bliss](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mr_Bliss "Mr Bliss")) with short black hair, thin lips and glittering emerald eyes. He often wears a suit and his right hand has a black glove that covers his skinless red hand that glistens with blood and wet muscle. In Seasons of War, the alternate dimension version of Serpine grows a beard, which according to [Tanith](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tanith_Low "Tanith Low") looks good on him.
