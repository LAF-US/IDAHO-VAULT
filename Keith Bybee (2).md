Keith Bybee is the LSO Staff Director / Budget and Policy Analysis who staffs both JFAC and EORAC, was CC'd on the March 2026 CCA letter, and in March 2024 unilaterally changed intent language in a budget bill after JFAC voted.

## Basic Info

## Bybee Incident (March 2024)

Unilaterally changed intent language in the Chinden Campus budget bill after JFAC voted, without co-chairs' knowledge. House passed the changed version. Bybee invoked JFAC Rule 9(b)(ii) ministerial-correction authority. Wendy Horman said she was unaware and would have brought any change back to the full committee. Reported by Clark Corbin, Idaho Capital Sun, March 28, 2024.
