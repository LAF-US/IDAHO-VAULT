**Count Olaf's associates** are, along with, the main antagonists of *[A Series of Unfortunate Events](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/A_Series_of_Unfortunate_Events "A Series of Unfortunate Events")*. Many of them are conspiring to plot a way to obtain the [Baudelaire Fortune](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Baudelaire_Fortune "Baudelaire Fortune"), although others follow Olaf for other motivations.

Many of them are on the fire-starting side of [VFD](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Volunteer_Fire_Department "Volunteer Fire Department"). Some of Olaf's associates are henchfolk, like the Hook-Handed Man, and some have other relations to him, such as Carmelita Spats being his adopted daughter.

Throughout the course of the series, Count Olaf has had a total of seventeen associates (but only five actual henchfolk);[^1] eventually each associate either left Olaf for or was killed.

By *[The End](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_End "The End")*, Count Olaf is working on his own, with no associates to assist him.

For the first seven books, in each book only one associate is helping Olaf, apart from *The Bad Beginning,* where they all appear. Many names as part of their disguises are anagrams of "Count Olaf".

## The Original "Theater Troupe"

### Fernald, the Hook-Handed Man

![Fernald, the Hook-Handed Man.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/snicket/images/9/98/Fernald.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20180216171236)

Fernald, the Hook-Handed Man.

[Fernald](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Fernald "Fernald") is the longest associate Count Olaf ever had. Fernald has hooks for hands and it is unknown how he lost his original hands, though it is implied to be through the [Anwhistle Aquatics](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Anwhistle_Aquatics "Anwhistle Aquatics") fire.[^2] He left him in *[The Grim Grotto](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Grim_Grotto "The Grim Grotto")* when he found his sister, [Fiona](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Fiona "Fiona"), in the *Queequeg* submarine, and the two stole *[The Carmelita](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Carmelita "The Carmelita")* from Olaf. His fate is left to the [Great Unknown](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Bombinating_Beast "Bombinating Beast").[^3]

### The Bald Man with the Long Nose

![Bald Man](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/snicket/images/a/a9/Bald_Man.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20120904204354)

[The Bald Man with the Long Nose](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bald_Man_with_the_Long_Nose "The Bald Man with the Long Nose") is one of Count Olaf's main five associates. Described to be as bald as an egg and as devious as an Egyptian King, the Bald Man is first seen by the [Baudelaire orphans](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Baudelaire_Family "Baudelaire Family") in [Count Olaf's house](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Olaf%27s_house "Count Olaf's house"). He later helps Olaf during his scheme at [Lucky Smells Lumbermill](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Lucky_Smells_Lumbermill "Lucky Smells Lumbermill") in [Paltryville](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Paltryville "Paltryville"), posing as [Foreman Flacutono](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Foreman_Flacutono "Foreman Flacutono"). Afterwards, he poses as a head doctor at [Heimlich Hospital](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Heimlich_Hospital "Heimlich Hospital") during another of his boss' schemes. Finally, the Bald Man meets his end at the hands of several [starving lions](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Volunteer_Feline_Detectives "Volunteer Feline Detectives") at [Caligari Carnival](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Caligari_Carnival "Caligari Carnival") along with the fortune teller [Madame Lulu](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Olivia_Caliban "Olivia Caliban").[^4]

### Henchperson Of Indeterminate Gender

![Orlando](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/snicket/images/9/9c/Orlando.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20120901190633)

The [Henchperson of Indeterminate Gender](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Henchperson_of_Indeterminate_Gender "Henchperson of Indeterminate Gender") is described as being extremely fat and of unknown gender. Snicket commonly refers to them as "the overweight accomplice." It is known that they have a very strange laugh, which sounds like a howl. In *[The Hostile Hospital](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Hostile_Hospital "The Hostile Hospital")*, the figure presumably met their death in the [fire that destroyed Heimlich Hospital](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Heimlich_Hospital_Fire "Heimlich Hospital Fire").[^5]

### The White-Faced Women

![White-Faced Women](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/snicket/images/e/e8/White-Faced_Women.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20090426235403)

The [White-Faced Women](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/White-Faced_Women "White-Faced Women") are described by [Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Lemony_Snicket "Lemony Snicket") as having powder on their faces. The two sisters assisted Count Olaf in a series of crimes, such as the kidnapping of the [Quagmire Triplets](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Quagmire_Family "Quagmire Family"). In *[The Slippery Slope](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Slippery_Slope "The Slippery Slope")*, the two become sick of Olaf's villainy and simply walk away from the group. Their current whereabouts are unknown.[^6]

### The Wart-Faced Man

The [Wart-Faced Man](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Wart-Faced_Man "Wart-Faced Man") made his first and only appearance in *[The Bad Beginning](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bad_Beginning "The Bad Beginning")* and is described as being important-looking. He was the member who turned off the lights in the theater so he, Olaf, and the troupe could escape. His current whereabouts are unknown.[^1]

### Unseen associates

In *The Bad Beginning*, there were several other people (at least 7) invited to the dinner party at [Count Olaf's house](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Olaf%27s_House "Count Olaf's House"). Although the Baudelaires could not see them, they promised to be just as frightening as the other henchmen. Their current whereabouts are unknown as they are not mentioned again, except in chapter 10 of [Lemony Snicket's Autobiography](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Lemony_Snicket:_The_Unauthorized_Autobiography "Lemony Snicket: The Unauthorized Autobiography"), which contains pages from Snicket's [commonplace book](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Commonplace_Book "Commonplace Book") sent to the [Valorous Farms Dairy.](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Valorous_Farms_Dairy "Valorous Farms Dairy")[^1]

**Appearances:**

## Other Associates

### Dr. Georgina Orwell

![Orwell](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/snicket/images/7/79/Orwell.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20170116160355)

[Georgina Orwell](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Georgina_Orwell "Georgina Orwell") was an optometrist and a hypnotist who only appeared in *[The Miserable Mill](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Miserable_Mill "The Miserable Mill")*. She hypnotized [Klaus](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Klaus_Baudelaire "Klaus Baudelaire") multiple times, using the words lucky and inordinate. Dr. Orwell was killed by the buzz saw when she stepped back into its path.[^7]

**Appearances:**

- *[The Miserable Mill](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Miserable_Mill "The Miserable Mill")*

### Esmé Squalor

!["I am Esme Gigi Genevieve Squalor!" ](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/snicket/images/c/c8/EsmeS.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/100?cb=20130519152957)

"I am Esme Gigi Genevieve Squalor!"

[Esmé Squalor](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Esm%C3%A9_Squalor "Esmé Squalor") first appeared in *[The Ersatz Elevator](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ersatz_Elevator "The Ersatz Elevator")*, wherein she helped Olaf with his plans to steal the Quagmire and Baudelaire fortunes. She revealed that she knew about Count Olaf's plan all along, after pushing the children into the ersatz elevator.[^8] In *[The Vile Village](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Vile_Village "The Vile Village")*, she becomes his girlfriend, and remains with him until *[The Penultimate Peril](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Penultimate_Peril "The Penultimate Peril")*, when Count Olaf fires her. She supposedly perished in the [Hotel Denouement](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Hotel_Denouement "Hotel Denouement") fire.[^9]

### Madame Lulu

![Madame Lulu (Olívia)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/snicket/images/f/f9/Madame_Lulu_%28Ol%C3%ADvia%29.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20120520153402)

[Madame Lulu](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Olivia_Caliban "Olivia Caliban") was an associate introduced in *[The Carnivorous Carnival](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Carnivorous_Carnival "The Carnivorous Carnival")*. She told Count Olaf where the Baudelaire children were each time they moved, as her goal was to always give people what they wanted, no matter the consequences. It is strongly implied that she was in love with Olaf, which is likely the reason that Esmé wanted her to be pushed into the lion's pit. She was eaten by lions and fell into the lions pit along with the Bald Man with the Long Nose.[^4] Her real name is Olivia Caliban,[^3] and she was a former Volunteer, as noted by [Captain Widdershins](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Captain_Widdershins "Captain Widdershins").[^2]

**Appearances:**

- *[The Carnivorous Carnival](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Carnivorous_Carnival "The Carnivorous Carnival")*
- *[The Grim Grotto](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Grim_Grotto "The Grim Grotto")* (mentioned)

### Hugo, Colette, and Kevin

[Hugo](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Hugo "Hugo"), [Colette](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Colette "Colette"), and [Kevin](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Kevin "Kevin") were three employees of the freakshow from [Caligari Carnival](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Caligari_Carnival "Caligari Carnival") who joined Count Olaf's troupe in *[The Carnivorous Carnival](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Carnivorous_Carnival "The Carnivorous Carnival")* due to being promised a better life.[^4] They assisted in the abduction of the Snow Scouts and in planning a poisoning at Hotel Denouement. It is unknown whether or not they survived the Hotel Denouement fire.[^9]

**Appearances:**

- *[The Carnivorous Carnival](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Carnivorous_Carnival "The Carnivorous Carnival")*

### Carmelita Spats

![Carmelita Spats](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/snicket/images/f/f0/Carmelita_Spats.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20111108193603)

[Carmelita Spats](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Carmelita_Spats "Carmelita Spats") first appeared in *[The Austere Academy](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Austere_Academy "The Austere Academy")*, but joined the Troupe in *[The Slippery Slope](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Slippery_Slope "The Slippery Slope")*, when Esmé adopted her.[^6] She named Olaf's submarine *[The Carmelita](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Carmelita "The Carmelita")* and performed tap-dancing ballerina fairy princess veterinarian dance recitals to torture the children rowing the ship.[^2] It is unknown whether she survived the Hotel Denouement Fire.[^9]

### The Sinister Duo

![43443891 745503982458768 7130670675240419328 n](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/snicket/images/3/3d/43443891_745503982458768_7130670675240419328_n.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20181027023413)

The [Man With a Beard But No Hair](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Man_With_a_Beard_But_No_Hair "Man With a Beard But No Hair") and the [Woman With Hair But No Beard](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Woman_With_Hair_But_No_Beard "Woman With Hair But No Beard") - or [Sinister Duo](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Sinister_Duo "Category:Sinister Duo") for short - are described as having "an aura of menace;" even Olaf and Esmé are afraid of the pair.[^6] The two first appear in *[The Slippery Slope](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Slippery_Slope "The Slippery Slope")* wherein they use V.F.D. Eagles to kidnap a group of [Snow Scouts](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Snow_Scouts "Snow Scouts"). The two later appear in *[The Penultimate Peril](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Penultimate_Peril "The Penultimate Peril")* as High Court Judges for the Baudelaires' trial. It is unknown whether the two survived the Hotel Denouement Fire.[^9]

**Appearances:**

- *[The Slippery Slope](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Slippery_Slope "The Slippery Slope")*
- *[The Penultimate Peril](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Penultimate_Peril "The Penultimate Peril")*

### Fiona

![Fiona](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/snicket/images/3/3c/Fiona.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20191217033435)

[Fiona](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Fiona "Fiona") joined Count Olaf's troupe briefly to stay with her brother, Fernald, and to find her stepfather, [Captain Widdershins](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Captain_Widdershins "Captain Widdershins").[^2] Very soon afterwards, she and Fernald double crossed Count Olaf and stole his submarine, *[The Carmelita](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Carmelita "The Carmelita"),* and the kidnapped recruits aboard.[^9] In *[The End](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_End "The End")*, [Kit Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Kit_Snicket "Kit Snicket") says that she was either killed or saved by [The Great Unknown](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Great_Unknown "The Great Unknown").

**Appearances:**

- *[The Grim Grotto](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Grim_Grotto "The Grim Grotto")*

## Minor associates

![, 2025 Edition.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/snicket/images/b/bf/TBB2025_Troupe.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20251018194643)

The Bad Beginning, 2025 Edition.

As listed in *[Lemony Snicket: The Unauthorized Autobiography](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Lemony_Snicket:_The_Unauthorized_Autobiography "Lemony Snicket: The Unauthorized Autobiography")*, Count Olaf's other associates are:

- [Ivan Lachrymose](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Ivan_Lachrymose "Ivan Lachrymose"), a lake explorer for whom [Lake Lachrymose](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Lake_Lachrymose "Lake Lachrymose") was named.
- [Esmé Squalor Fan Club](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Esm%C3%A9_Squalor_Fan_Club "Esmé Squalor Fan Club")
- Six unknown enemies
- An Old Count and his only son
- Three Professors
- [Lena Pukalie](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Lena_Pukalie "Lena Pukalie"), a film critic
- "A man's life"
- Firestarting side of [VFD](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Volunteer_Fire_Department "Volunteer Fire Department")

He uses this to calculate that Count Olaf had at least twenty-five associates, possibly more than forty-one.[^10]

- Count Olaf also mentions a woman named [Monday](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Monday "Monday") as an associate of his.[^3]
