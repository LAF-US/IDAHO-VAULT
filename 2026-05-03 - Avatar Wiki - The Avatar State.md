This page is the episode page for "The Avatar State", the episode where the
mechanics of the Avatar State are explained in-world. Roku describes the
Avatar State as a defense mechanism that gives the Avatar the skills and
knowledge of past lives, while warning that death in the Avatar State breaks
the reincarnation cycle.
