The **Anwhistle family** consist in the brothers [Gregor](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Gregor_Anwhistle "Gregor Anwhistle") and [Ike Anwhistle](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Ike_Anwhistle "Ike Anwhistle"), and their parents. During his life, Ike was married to [Josephine Anwhistle](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Josephine_Anwhistle "Josephine Anwhistle").

## Members

- Gregor and Ike's Parents
- [Gregor Anwhistle](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Gregor_Anwhistle "Gregor Anwhistle") †
- [Ike Anwhistle](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Ike_Anwhistle "Ike Anwhistle") †
- [Josephine Anwhistle](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Josephine_Anwhistle "Josephine Anwhistle") †

Little is known about Gregor and Ike's mother. Josephine stated that she had one eyebrow and one ear,[^1] which was also a feature of a student that [Ishmael](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Ishmael "Ishmael") had. If these are the same character, it means that she is an orphan and has siblings, and was at some point concerned about being poisoned by her guardian.[^2] She is Beatrice’s first cousin.

Gregor was an ichnologist who founded [Anwhistle Aquatics](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Anwhistle_Aquatics "Anwhistle Aquatics") and studied the [Medusoid Mycelium](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Medusoid_Mycelium "Medusoid Mycelium") there before [Fernald](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Fernald "Fernald") and [Captain Widdershins](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Captain_Widdershins "Captain Widdershins") burned the center down. Gregor died in the fire.[^3]

Ike was married to Josephine Anwhistle, and the two lived in a [house](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Josephine_Anwhistle%27s_House "Josephine Anwhistle's House") on a cliff overlooking [Lake Lachrymose](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Lake_Lachrymose "Lake Lachrymose"). Both were attacked, on two separate occasions, by [Lachrymose Leeches](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Lachrymose_Leech "Lachrymose Leech").

[Beatrice Baudelaire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Beatrice_Baudelaire "Beatrice Baudelaire") shared the Anwhistle family trait of being able to whistle with crackers in her mouth.[^1]

Gregor, Ike and Josephine were known [VFD](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Volunteer_Fire_Department "Volunteer Fire Department") Volunteers.

### Theories

It is possible that Gregor and Ike are on the [Snicket family tree](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Snicket_Clan "Snicket Clan") as [G.](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/G._Snicket "G. Snicket") and [I. Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/I._Snicket "I. Snicket"); their mother, Josephine's mother-in-law, had only one eyebrow and one ear,[^1] which was also a feature of a student that [Ishmael](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Ishmael "Ishmael") had.[^2] Seeing as [Jacques Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Jacques_Snicket "Jacques Snicket") also had a unibrow,[^4] this could be a family trait.

[D. Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/D._Snicket "D. Snicket"), on the family tree, has three children; [G](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/G._Snicket "G. Snicket"), [H](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/H._Snicket "H. Snicket"), and [I](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/I._Snicket "I. Snicket"). If G and I on the Snicket family tree are Gregor and Ike, the H on the tree is a sibling of theirs; the gender, whereabouts and status of this sibling is unknown, though they are often theorized to be [Hector](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Hector "Hector").[^5]

## Trivia

- The family's surname comes from a pun; when said fast, [Ike Anwhistle](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Ike_Anwhistle "Ike Anwhistle") 's name is "I can whistle," referencing his and [Beatrice Baudelaire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Beatrice_Baudelaire "Beatrice Baudelaire") 's shared ability to whistle with crackers in their mouth.[^1]
