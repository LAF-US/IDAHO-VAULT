The Library from the Netflix Show.

**[Dewey Denouement](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Dewey_Denouement "Dewey Denouement")'s underwater library**
is located beneath the pond in front of
[Hotel Denouement](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Hotel_Denouement "Hotel Denouement").

## Description

The library contains a vast collection of evidence that Dewey and his
"comrade," most likely [Kit Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Kit_Snicket "Kit Snicket"),
have gathered over the years. It is said to contain information about every
person, place, thing, and event associated with
[V.F.D.](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/V.F.D._\(Volunteer_Fire_Department\) "V.F.D. (Volunteer Fire Department)").

According to Dewey, all of this information is archived in underwater rooms
organized like "a mirror image of the hotel itself." For that reason, the
library is presented as one of the best chances for prosecuting the
fire-starting side of V.F.D.

Although the hotel is destroyed, Lemony mentions that the underwater library
has remained safe all these years.

In the TV series, Dewey claims that his library contains information gathered
by every kind of V.F.D. participant: agent, scholar, researcher, inventor,
scientist, explorer, cartographer, poet, journalist, naturalist,
herpetologist, optometrist, receptionist, chef, waiter, taxi driver, sea
captain, film director, children's book author, and mountaineer. He mentions
this to the Baudelaire orphans while they walk through the
[V.F.D. tunnel](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/V.F.D._tunnel "V.F.D. tunnel").
