**The Empty Lands** are a realm north of
[the Great Rift](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/The_Great_Rift "The Great Rift")
in the *[Old Kingdom series](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Kingdom_series "Old Kingdom series")*.

They are described as a seemingly endless wasteland containing no Life, no
[Charter](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Charter_magic "Charter magic"),
and no air.
[Sabriel](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Sabriel "Sabriel") believes
the region may be a world destroyed by
[Orannis](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Orannis "Orannis"), which
supports the idea that some of the series' "countries" are in fact different
worlds.

[Spirit-glass](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Spirit-glass "Spirit-glass")
shards can be found there. [Free Magic sorcerers](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Free_magic_sorcerers "Free magic sorcerers")
sometimes rush into the Empty Lands, seize as many shards as they can, and flee
again; many die in the attempt.

[Lirael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Lirael "Lirael") and
[Nicholas Sayre](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Nicholas_Sayre "Nicholas Sayre")
later enter the Empty Lands inside a Charter bubble, possible only because Nick
is a source of the Charter. There they find and destroy
[Chlorr of the Mask](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Chlorr_of_the_Mask "Chlorr of the Mask")'s
original corpse, escaping with only minutes to spare.
