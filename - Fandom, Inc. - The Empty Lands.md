The Empty Lands, likely created by Orannis. Art by Alan Burley.

**The Empty Lands** are a realm directly north from [The Great Rift](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/The_Great_Rift "The Great Rift").[^1] A seemingly endless wasteland, it contains no Life, no [Charter](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Charter_magic "Charter magic"), or even any air. [Sabriel](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Sabriel "Sabriel") believes it is a world once destroyed by [Orannis](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Orannis "Orannis"), furthering the concept that different "countries" in the [series](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Kingdom_series "Old Kingdom series") are in fact different worlds.[^2]

[Spirit-glass](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Spirit-glass "Spirit-glass") shards can be found in The Empty Lands. [Free Magic sorcerers](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Free_magic_sorcerers "Free magic sorcerers") attempt to rush in, grab as many as possible, and rush out; many die in the attempt.

[Lirael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Lirael "Lirael") and [Nicholas Sayre](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Nicholas_Sayre "Nicholas Sayre") use a Charter-bubble (only possible because Nick is a source of the Charter) to enter The Empty Lands and find and destroy [Chlorr of the Mask's](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Chlorr_of_the_Mask "Chlorr of the Mask") original corpse. They succeed with only minutes to spare.
