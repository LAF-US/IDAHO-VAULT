American poet, performer, spoken word artist. Goes by **Rives** professionally. Born 1968. Appeared on HBO's *Def Poetry Jam* seasons 3–6; member of Team Hollywood (2004 National Poetry Slam champions). Six TED talks.

- The 4 a.m. Mystery — TED2007; satirical conspiracy scholarship on 4 AM as a culturally charged hour
- The Museum of Four in the Morning — follow-up TED talk; expanded the 4 AM collection after audience submissions flooded in post-TED2007
