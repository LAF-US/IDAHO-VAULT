[[Rives]] — TED2007. ~8 minutes.

Satirical conspiracy talk presenting 4:00 AM as a culturally haunted hour. Rives assembled references from *The Simpsons*, Bill Clinton's autobiography, and other cultural artifacts as mock "evidence" of a pattern. Framed as expert testimony on something deliberately inconsequential.

Went viral. Audience began sending Rives more 4 AM references from art, literature, and culture — spawning [[The Museum of Four in the Morning]].
