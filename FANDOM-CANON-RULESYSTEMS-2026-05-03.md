# FANDOM Canon Rulesystem Registry

This registry turns the vault's literary canon captures into a checkable ruleset
inventory. The goal is not to flatten the worlds into one schema. The goal is
to keep each worldbuilding system legible, inspectable, and enforceable in the
vault.

## Canon list

### Bartimaeus

Bartimaeus is a **binding ontology**:

- true names define control surfaces
- summoning and dismissal define the instance lifecycle
- levels define scale and hierarchy
- forms define the current manifestation
- masters define the contractual relation
- Essence and the Other Place define the spirit substrate

The vault uses Bartimaeus as its strongest reference for instance identity,
binding, and summon/release mechanics.

### Snicket / ASOUE

Snicket is a **family-institution index**:

- alphabetical naming encodes inheritance pressure
- triplets and hidden thirds destabilize binary reading
- V.F.D. behaves like a covert institution
- Hotel Denouement and Dewey's library turn architecture into a record system
- tragedy is preserved through clue chains, indices, and archives

The vault uses Snicket for family roles, hidden-third structures, and indexing
as narrative architecture.

### Children of the Lamp

Children of the Lamp is a **tribe-vessel law system**:

- tribes encode alignment and obligation
- lamps and bottles are vessels with legal states
- wishes consume limited power
- seals and bindings determine coercion
- heat, luck, and entry protocol affect what the vessel does

The vault uses Children of the Lamp for persona-vessel states, time-skewed
containment, and inhabited chamber logic.

### Old Kingdom

Old Kingdom is a **Charter / Free Magic law system**:

- the Charter constrains and orders reality through symbols
- Free Magic is unconstrained and corrosive
- Charter Marks are the access grammar
- bloodlines and Charter Stones are institutional supports
- Abhorsens and bells are the enforcement layer for the Dead

The vault uses Old Kingdom for symbolic law, boundary enforcement, and
structured magical opposition.

### Skulduggery Pleasant

Skulduggery Pleasant is a **hidden-society discipline system**:

- the Sanctuary and allied groups govern the magical world
- Adept disciplines define power classes
- necromancy is an object- and death-energy discipline
- the Faceless Ones and Remnant introduce external threat and possession
- teleporter and Book of Names mechanics show object-bound control surfaces

The vault uses Skulduggery Pleasant for concealed institutions, discipline
classes, and adversarial governance.

### Avatar

Avatar is a **spirit-cycle continuity system**:

- the Avatar Spirit carries continuity across incarnations
- reincarnation moves the active Avatar through the nation cycle
- the Avatar State exposes accumulated skills and knowledge from past lives
- past-life continuity can be weakened, severed, or queried when the bridge is intact
- Wan and Raava define the origin fusion between human vessel and spirit
- coercive triggering shows the danger of forcing state access without stable integration

The vault uses Avatar for succession, incarnation, continuity-carrier logic,
and the distinction between an active instance and the inherited memory chain.

## Intended enforcement

The registry is meant to be machine-checked. Each canon record should keep:

- a canonical display name
- a rulesystem label
- a non-empty primitive list
- a source file set that exists in the vault

That keeps the canon corpus usable as vaulted code instead of only as reading
material.
