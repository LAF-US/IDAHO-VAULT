## Powerful Practices

The Wassmuth Powerful Practices form a comprehensive human rights framework made up of six interconnected behaviors that help individuals and communities honor human rights through everyday actions. This framework turns shared values into practical, repeatable ways of treating one another with care and fairness. Together, the six practices form an integrated system, each supporting the others to help people understand how their choices and actions can intentionally uphold human rights.

**Collect Joy**: Notice, savor, and share moments of connection, warmth, and delight as expressions of human dignity and belonging. Paying attention to what is life-giving cultivates hope and sustains commitment to building a better world for all.

**Design for Belonging**: Shape environments, experiences, and systems so all people are included, valued, and able to participate fully. By intentionally setting the stage for belonging and designing for key moments during interactions, justice is made visible.

**Listen Deeply**: Offer your full presence to another person without interruption, judgment, or trying to fix. Seeking to understand feelings, perspectives, and needs affirms that every person matters.

**Stay Curious**: Approach people, situations, and ideas with openness and a willingness to learn. Embracing the unknown with humility and wonder creates space for understanding complexity, holding multiple perspectives, and discovering new possibilities.

**Notice and Name Bright Spots**: Identify, affirm, and amplify strengths, progress, and positive patterns. Highlighting what works builds confidence and momentum for sustainable change.

**Align Actions With Values**: Pause to reflect on foundational human rights commitments and intentionally choose actions that honor dignity, diversity, equality, and joy. This guides ethical decision-making, accountability, and repair, especially in moments of conflict or complexity.

Through these six practices, individuals and communities can reduce harm, challenge inequity, repair relationships, and strengthen systems that honor the dignity of all people. This framework helps people understand how everyday choices determine whether human rights are upheld or violated. The Wassmuth Powerful Practices turn human rights from abstract ideals into concrete, daily actions that empower people to create a more just and joyful world, one choice at a time.
