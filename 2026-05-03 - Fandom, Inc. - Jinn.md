The Jinn are a good-djinn tribe aligned toward increasing luck in the world.

The page presents them as one of the three good tribes that oppose the bad tribes' chaos. It is a compact tribe page, but it still anchors the series' moral geography by showing that the djinn world is divided into structured alignments rather than a single generic species.
