John Rives — follow-up to The 4 a.m. Mystery (TED2007).

After the original talk circulated online, people flooded Rives with 4 AM references from across art, literature, film, and culture. He built a conceptual museum from the submissions. The follow-up talk presents that collection — a crowd-sourced archive of 4 AM as a liminal cultural moment.

Nearly 3 million combined views across both talks.
