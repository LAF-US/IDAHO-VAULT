**Baron Vengeous** [^1] was one of [Mevolent](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent "Mevolent") 's infamous [Three Generals](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Three_Generals "The Three Generals"). Following the death of [Nefarian Serpine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Nefarian_Serpine "Nefarian Serpine"), he attempted to bring back [the Faceless Ones](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Faceless_Ones "The Faceless Ones"), whom he worshiped.

## Biography

### The War

During [the War](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_War "The War"), Vengeous was one of [Mevolent's Three Generals](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Three_Generals "The Three Generals"). He was the leader of the fanatical [Diablerie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Diablerie "Diablerie"), a cult dedicated to bringing the [Faceless Ones](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Faceless_Ones "Faceless Ones") back through any means necessary. It was mentioned that the [Torment](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Torment "Torment"), despite harbouring a dislike for both sides of [the War](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_War "The War"), decided that Vengeous was the worst of two evils. [The Torment](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Torment "The Torment") set out to kill Vengeous. However, sometime after 1861, Vengeous had a legendary battle with [Skulduggery Pleasant](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant"), in which Vengeous was defeated. Skulduggery Pleasant threw a stick of dynamite at Vengeous, winning the battle. Vengeous was then taken to a prison facility in Russia. The Torment, with Vengeous safely locked away, saw no reason to continue hunting the man. [Cu Gealach](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Cu_Gealach "Cu Gealach")

Before being caught, Vengeous was tasked with resurrecting a Faceless Ones using remains found in a long-forgotten tomb. Vengeous combined the remains with the limbs and organs of other creatures to form a hybrid called the Grotesquery. He was caught before he could finish it, and was missing two crucial ingredients: a Necromancer’s power to revive it, and another ingredient to keep the Grotesquery alive. When Lord Vile disappeared, presumed dead, Vengeous tried to find his armour, but could not.

Earlier in the war, Vengeous encountered [Saracen Rue](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Saracen_Rue "Saracen Rue") in Leeds. He also fought at [The Battle of Black Rock](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Black_Rock "The Battle of Black Rock"), and was driven into retreat by [Ghastly Bespoke's mother](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Ghastly_Bespoke%27s_mother "Ghastly Bespoke's mother").

### Playing With Fire

Baron Vengous escapes his confinement facility in Russia. It is a violent escape, resulting in the deaths of nine Cleavers and approximately a third of the prisoners there. The only clue as to how he escaped is that one of the walls is cracked, as a result of Billy-Ray Sanguine’s tunnelling power.

After escaping jail, Vengeous returns to Ireland, strangling to death a [Sanctuary](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Sanctuary "Sanctuary") agent who recognises him. He meets with the vampire Dusk, and tells him to wait outside the Sanctuary tonight, follow Skulduggery and Valkyrie Cain when they leave, and capture Valkyrie alive when he has the chance.

He joins together with [Dusk](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Dusk "Dusk"), a [vampire](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire "Vampire") who owed Vengeous an undisclosed debt, and [Billy-Ray Sanguine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Billy-Ray_Sanguine "Billy-Ray Sanguine"). Using the two men as his chief henchmen, Vengeous would begin to enact his plan to revive [the Grotesquery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Grotesquery "The Grotesquery").

Vengeous is revealed to be in search of [Lord Vile's](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Lord_Vile "Lord Vile") armour and [Valkyrie Cain's blood](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain"). Using both, he could revive [the Grotesquery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Grotesquery "The Grotesquery"). [The Grotesquery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Grotesquery "The Grotesquery") is made with the body parts of many other beasts grafted onto the torso of a [Faceless One](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Faceless_Ones "Faceless Ones"). This connection to [the Faceless Ones](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Faceless_Ones "Faceless Ones") was purported to be able to herald the return of the remaining gods.

Using an army of [Infected](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Infected "Infected"), Vengeous is able to find the fabled, powerful armour of [Lord Vile](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Lord_Vile "Lord Vile"), a fellow General. The immensely powerful Necromancer magic embedded in the armour falls under Vengeous' control. The magic is so powerful, he must wear a protective clothing underneath the armour. He also finds the body of the Grotesquery, a spectacle that is observed by [Valkyrie Cain](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") and [Skulduggery Pleasant](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant"). They witness Vengeous' sadistic power first hand: he uses his magic to rupture the body of an Infected who had dropped Grotesquery's body.

Valkyrie is captured while Skulduggery attempts to contact the [Sanctuary](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Sanctuary "Sanctuary") for reinforcements. After driving to a derelict church, Vengeous orders [Billy-Ray Sanguine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Billy-Ray_Sanguine "Billy-Ray Sanguine") to cut Valkyrie's hand. Her blood falls upon the Grotesquery. Valkyrie's blood, which contains traces of the [Faceless Ones'](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Faceless_Ones "Faceless Ones") power, is able to revive the Grotesquery. However, Skulduggery returns, foiling the ritual before it can be completed. The Grotesquery appears to be unable to be resurrected. Skulduggery and Valkyrie escape with the carcas of the Grotesquery, infuriating Vengeous.

It later awakens in the laboratory of [Kenspeckle Grouse](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Kenspeckle_Grouse "Kenspeckle Grouse"), enough of the ritual having been completed. It kills two of [Kenspeckle Grouse](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Kenspeckle_Grouse "Kenspeckle Grouse") 's assistants, [Stentor](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Stentor "Stentor") and [Civet](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Civet "Civet"), as well as three [Cleavers](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Cleaver "Cleaver") who attempt to stop it. After severely wounding [Tanith Low](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tanith_Low "Tanith Low"), the Grotesquery escapes, falling under Vengeous' command.

Vengeous, with the help of Sanguine, Dusk, and [Springheeled Jack](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Springheeled_Jack "Springheeled Jack"), orchestrates attacks on personnel from the International [Sanctuaries](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Sanctuary "Sanctuary"). While the Sanctuaries are preoccupied dealing with attacks, Vengeous rightfully assumes that they will be too busy to answer Ireland's request for aid, ensuring that Vengeous is not overwhelmed by the international magic community

It is discovered that Vengeous harbours a grudge against [China Sorrows](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/China_Sorrows "China Sorrows"), former leader of the Diablerie, because she defected from Vengeous' group. Sorrows realises this, and employs two elite bodyguards, [Zephyr](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Zephyr "Zephyr") and [Sev](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Sev "Sev"), to protect her from Vengeous' fury. Vengeous confronts China and her bodyguards in a car park, easily killing Sev and ripping apart Zephyr. He wonders how Lord Vile was ever defeated while wielding such power, and tells China that while Necromancy is a little crude, nothing Beats the sheer thrill of using it against one’s opponents. China mocks him, saying that he is farweaker than Lord Vile. She states “ [Lord Vile](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Lord_Vile "Lord Vile")... was different... he could have changed the world... you merely wear his clothes”. Baron Vengeous captures China, intending for her to watch the return of the Faceless Ones.

Vengeous then moves to [Clearwater Hospital](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Clearwater_Hospital "Clearwater Hospital"), a clandestine, abandoned site where he intends to bring [the Faceless Ones](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Faceless_Ones "Faceless Ones") back. Much of Vengeous' support has withered away by this point; Valkyrie Cain leads his army of Infected into the sea, and [Springheeled Jack](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Springheeled_Jack "Springheeled Jack") defects, incapacitating Dusk. Vengeous has the sole support of Sanguine, and the Grotesquery. Sanguine is captured at Clearwater by a Sanctuary task force consisting of [Skulduggery Pleasant](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant"), [Valkyrie Cain](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain"), [Mr. Bliss](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Bliss "Mr. Bliss"), [Tanith Low](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tanith_Low "Tanith Low"), and a multitude of [Cleavers](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Cleaver "Cleaver"). The group observes that Sanguine seemed to have ulterior motives in supporting Vengeous after over-hearing his phone call.

![Vengeous with the Grotesquery](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/skulduggery/images/9/91/Unt5.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20141202094025)

Vengeous with the Grotesquery

[The Grotesquery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Grotesquery "The Grotesquery") is then found alone, Vengeous having seemingly vanished. [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant"), [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain"), [Mr. Bliss](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Bliss "Mr. Bliss"), and [Tanith](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tanith_Low "Tanith Low") begin the initial attack, managing to wound [the Grotesquery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Grotesquery "The Grotesquery") before is it able to reach the peak of its power. It is then swarmed by the [Cleavers](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Cleaver "Cleaver"), who severely wound [the Grotesquery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Grotesquery "The Grotesquery"). Vengeous returns with the captured [China](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/China_Sorrows "China Sorrows"), using [Vile's armour](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Lord_Vile%27s_armour "Lord Vile's armour") to render [Tanith](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tanith_Low "Tanith Low") and the remaining [Cleavers](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Cleaver "Cleaver") unconscious. [The Torment](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Torment "The Torment") is called for reinforcement, but he too is defeated by the recovering [Grotesquery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Grotesquery "The Grotesquery"). With the group seemingly defeated, it appears as though Vengeous would emerge victorious. However, [China](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/China_Sorrows "China Sorrows") recovers, and fights Vengeous, allowing [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") and [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") to escape.

[Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain") and [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") flee to [the Bentley](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/1954_Bentley_R-Type_Continental "1954 Bentley R-Type Continental"), where they open the boot to reveal their secret weapon. Using [Sanguine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Billy-Ray_Sanguine "Billy-Ray Sanguine") as a mode of transport, they rise from the ground right next to Baron Vengeous. Not needing to deal with all the shadows at such close range, they remove [his armor](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Lord_Vile%27s_armour "Lord Vile's armour"), leaving Vengeous severely weakened. [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") shoots Vengeous below the heart, to which Vengeous protests, saying that he can’t die to [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant"); [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") is an abomination. [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant") responds that he’s a lot of things.

Vengeous, staring defeat in the face, crawls over to [the Grotesquery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Grotesquery "The Grotesquery") to apologise for failing to protect it. [The Grotesquery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Grotesquery "The Grotesquery") snaps his neck. [The Grotesquery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/The_Grotesquery "The Grotesquery") is then defeated by [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain"), but its death scream alerts [the Faceless Ones](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant:_The_Faceless_Ones "Skulduggery Pleasant: The Faceless Ones") to Earth's location.

### Alternate Dimension

Vengeous is present in Mevolent's universe. However, in peacetime, Vengeous little more than a prison guard to unruly mortals and mages. Vengeous is married to [Eliza Scorn](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Eliza_Scorn "Eliza Scorn"), a fanatic who has gone insane after briefly looking at a Faceless One. Also, he shaved his beard.

When Valkyrie Cain is forced into his dimension by [Silas Nadir](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Silas_Nadir "Silas Nadir"), a [Dimensional Shunter](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Dimensional_Shunter "Dimensional Shunter"), Vengeous is able to capture her. He suspects her to be a member of a feeble resistance movement. He takes her into the capital city of [Mevolent](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mevolent "Mevolent"), showing her a world with a stark contrast to her own. For instance, he shows her the naked, desecrated body of Mr. Bliss, used by Mevolent as a deterrent against uprising. He then brings her to Mevolent himself before leaving.

Later during the plot of the book, Skulduggery and Valkyrie return to Vengeous' dimension, where they infiltrate Mevolent's castle, using Eliza Scorn and [Nefarian Serpine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Nefarian_Serpine "Nefarian Serpine"). Vengeous, however, finds them, attacking Skulduggery and nearly rupturing Valkyrie's body. Skulduggery and the others escape with the [Sceptre of the Ancients](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Scepter_of_the_Ancients "Scepter of the Ancients").

Vengeous reappears during the battle of Ratoath, where Mevolent's forces attempted to crush the Resistance, once and for all. Vengeous pursues and attempts to kill [China Sorrows](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/China_Sorrows "China Sorrows"), the Resistance's leader, but is killed when [Anton Shudder](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Anton_Shudder "Anton Shudder"), her bodyguard, breaks his neck, much to Darquesse’s amusement,

When the Leibniz Universe is re-visited it is revealed that he has been brought back to life by Mevolent. He later attacks Skulduggery, Valkyrie, and Ravel, along with Vile and Mevolent. His face is described as slack and lifeless, and his expression never changes, even when strangling Ravel.

Following the draugr outbreak, Vengeous is dispatched to America where the epidemic began. He is trapped inside a fort by the Necromancers but is unbale to take his own life, due to his resurrection making him immortal. He is eventually killed upon the arrival of [Valkyrie](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie "Valkyrie"), [Skulduggery](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery "Skulduggery"), [Tanith](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Tanith_Low "Tanith Low"), [Serpine](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Serpine "Serpine"), [Saracen](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Saracen_Rue "Saracen Rue") and [Dexter Vex](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Dexter_Vex "Dexter Vex").

## Physical Appearance

He was described as tall and muscular with gun-metal grey toned hair and a tightly cropped beard. He was also described as militaristic.

#### Alternate Dimension

In the alternate reality, his garb was unchanged, but he had shaved his beard, and no longer carried his [cutlass](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Baron_Vengeous%27_cutlass "Baron Vengeous' cutlass").

## Magic and Abilities

### Magic

Vengeous subscribes to an adept discipline that allows him to make objects rupture and break at a glance. His eyes turn yellow when he uses his ability.

### Necromancy

After obtaining [Lord Vile's armour](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Lord_Vile%27s_armour "Lord Vile's armour"), Vengeous gains a wide variety of necromancer abilities such as controlling shadows. He demonstrates a great prowess for this ability, although he is nowhere near as powerful as Lord Vile was. He is able to use the ability to great effect on [Skulduggery Pleasant](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Skulduggery_Pleasant "Skulduggery Pleasant"), [Valkyrie Cain](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Valkyrie_Cain "Valkyrie Cain"), [Mr Bliss](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Mr_Bliss "Mr Bliss") and killing many [Cleavers](https://skulduggery.fandom.com/wiki/Cleavers "Cleavers").

Abilities

He is well trained with his cutlass and is said to be even faster than Skulduggery.
