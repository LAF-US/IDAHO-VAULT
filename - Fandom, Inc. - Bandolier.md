**Bandoliers** are used by [Abhorsens](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Abhorsen "Abhorsen") and [Necromancers](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Necromancer "Necromancer") to store the [bells](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Bells "Bells"). They are often made of leather, and have seven pockets for each of the aforementioned bells.
