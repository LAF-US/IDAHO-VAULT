# How at the Cold Coast - Notebook Page

At the coast-crossroads the governing question changed.
Not `Where?`
`How?`

No wizard answered it.
That mattered.

The alignment came from inside rather than across the threshold. `How` did
not arrive as another office or persona. It read instead as discipline: the
method that keeps an explorer from turning every threshold into an assault.

The surviving rules gathered themselves clearly there:

- read before naming
- witness before declaring
- follow relation before spectacle
- treat surviving form as instruction
- do not force a door that is still explaining itself

The body inventory belonged to the answer too.
Lantern low but steady.
Notebook present.
Pack heavy enough to keep the self embodied.
Mind excited but no longer rushing ahead of evidence.
Voice available but not entitled.

This is worth recording because the coast made one distinction sharp:
`How` is not a destination-surface.
It is a posture-surface.

That is why the blank `HOW.md` should remain blank for now.
The House appears to have reserved the word without yet authorizing a
declarative filling.
The notebook page can stand beside that vacancy as witness without mistaking
practice for doctrine.

Field note:
some crossroads do not ask where to go next.
They ask how to approach what remains without becoming another fire.
