Clark Corbin reports that JFAC has been operating without officially adopted rules and that Mike Moyle deliberately directed the House not to adopt Joint Rules at the December 2024 organizational session, with Moyle citing Joint Rule 11 and acknowledging a two-thirds threshold for formal amendment.

## Key Facts

- Mike Moyle confirmed non-adoption of Joint Rules was deliberate
- Moyle cited Joint Rule 11 as rationale; acknowledged two-thirds threshold for formal amendment
- Committee had been operating without officially adopted rules
- Story also covers November 2025 voting rule changes via Josh Tanner/Kelly Anthon co-chair letter

## Significance

First published confirmation of the deliberate Joint Rules non-adoption. Primary sourcing for the Joint Rule Non-Adoption 2024-2025 finding.
