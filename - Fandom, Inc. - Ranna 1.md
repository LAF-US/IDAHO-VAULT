**Ranna** is the first of the [Nine Bright Shiners](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Nine_Bright_Shiners "Nine Bright Shiners"). In [The Beginning](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/The_Beginning "The Beginning"), Ranna became part of the [Charter.](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/The_Charter "The Charter") As a [bell](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Bells "Bells"), Ranna is the Sleeper, who brings sleep to those who hear it. It was wielded by [Touchstone](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Torrigan "Torrigan") at the binding of [Orannis](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Orannis "Orannis").

## Bell Sound

Ranna has a sweet, low, and lazy sound; a muffled sleepy peal that carries the sensations of falling asleep.[^1]

## Uses

### Terciel and Elinor

- Pipe blown by Terciel during the attack on Coldhallow house.
- Rung by Terciel and Tizanael during their battle with Kerrigor.

### Sabriel

- Sabriel uses Ranna while in death retrieving Touchstone's spirit.[^2]
- Sabriel uses Ranna to quell a Mordaut hiding among villagers who have taken refuge from the dead on an island.[^3]
- Rung by Sabriel after binding Kerrigor with Mogget's collar.[^4]

## Trivia

- Ranna was used to bind [Mogget](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Mogget "Mogget") at the end of [*Sabriel*](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Sabriel_\(Book\) "Sabriel (Book)"), as [Saraneth](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Saraneth "Saraneth") had been destroyed by [Kerrigor](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Kerrigor "Kerrigor").
- Wearing [Charter](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Charter_magic "Charter magic") -spelled protective garments and armor can help to fight off Ranna's effects, as seen when it was used by an enemy [necromancer](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Necromancer "Necromancer") in *[Goldenhand](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Goldenhand_\(Book\) "Goldenhand (Book)").*
- Mogget claims Ranna holds Kerrigor "more tightly than she holds me," implying her original form was female.
- Ranna is one of three bells [Idrach](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Idrach "Idrach") forges in [Death](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death").
