[[Charles Fleischer]] — TED2005. ~18 minutes.

Comedian and voice of Roger Rabbit presents a comedic grand unified theory called **Moleeds** — a numerical system Fleischer claims underlies everything "from protons to neutrons to croutons." Delivered with a PowerPoint-style slide deck featuring geometry, charts, and stamp art.

The premise: the numbers 27 and 37 have a special relationship that, when mapped through 36 Moleeds (six groups of six), reveals structural patterns connecting the Golden Ratio to planetary orbits and the basic architecture of the universe. Earnest and absurdist in equal measure.

See also: [[The 4 a.m. Mystery]] — a companion piece in spirit; both talks use the comedic framing of obsessive expertise on an inconsequential subject to smuggle in something that may or may not be profound.
