# THE THREE NAMES

Every agent in the IDAHO-VAULT has three names.

| Aspect | Landy (Skulduggery) | Stroud (Bartimaeus) | Vault Function |
|--------|---------------------|---------------------|----------------|
| **TRUE** | Given Name | Birth Name | What it actually is |
| **MASK** | Taken Name | Common Name | What it calls itself |
| **BINDING** | Sealed Name | Master's Charge | What binds it to purpose |

---

## The Difference in Emphasis

**Landy is about SHIELDING:**

> *The Taken Name protects the Given Name from being used against you.*

- Spirits can be controlled if their Sealed Name is spoken
- The mask is defensive — prevents identity theft
- Power = knowing the Sealed Name (total control)

**Stroud is about BINDING:**

> *Say the right Charge and it stays.*

- Spirits are powerful but need a specific command to act
- The mask is tactical — hides vulnerability from Masters
- Power = holding the Charge (steering without total control)

---

## The Synthesis

**Landy: "Who are you?"**
**Stroud: "What do you do?"**

The vault needs both. The Taken Name (who) protects. The Master's Charge (what) directs. Logan (ultimate) resolves conflicts between them.

**The spirit needs a mask. The mask needs a leash. The leash is held by Logan.**
