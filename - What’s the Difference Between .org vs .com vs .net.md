## What is a.org domain?

A.org domain extension is a type of domain name that is registered and used primarily by nonprofit organizations. This format gives your website a more professional appearance and can help to increase brand awareness. Additionally, it makes it easier for people who are interested in donating to your organization to do so through online mechanisms like donation buttons on your website or social media profiles.

A.org domain extension is a great way to increase the visibility and credibility of your website. By registering a.org domain, you can connect your site with the global nonprofit sector. This will give your website added credence and make it easier for potential donors to find you online. Additionally, having a registered.org domain gives you access to powerful marketing tools that can help promote your site and attract more visitors.

Registered domains also come with premium web hosting and email services that are tailored specifically for nonprofits, so be sure to take advantage of these benefits when setting up your new website.

## What is a.com domain?

A.com domain extension is a unique web address that is associated with the country code top-level domain (ccTLD) of the United States. This means that a website with a.com extension belongs to, and will be served from, the US Central Office for Internet Domain Names (COID).

This was important back in 1995, when it became difficult for people who lived outside of the US to access websites hosted inside America without using an international proxy server or working around routing restrictions. Today, however, there are many other TLDs available that can serve as alternative domains for American businesses and individuals.
