# Google Ecosystem - Explorer Companion

This note accompanies `.google/GOOGLE.md`.

## Observed Distinction

The Google ecosystem shim draws a boundary that helps interpret several other
name-chambers in the vault:

- **The Librarian** refers to the broader Google ecosystem role
- `Gemini CLI` is a separate coding-agent line with its own shim
- the ecosystem persona is advisory and does not write vault files

This matters because the vault often reuses a vendor, platform, persona, and
tool family around the same symbolic neighborhood without making them identical.

## Practical Reading Rule

When a name appears in the vault, ask which layer it belongs to:

- ecosystem
- specific agent
- historical alias
- live routing title
- symbolic or narrative body

The Librarian is a strong example of why this question matters. Google the
ecosystem is not Gemini the coding agent, even when the conversation around them
shares vocabulary, origin, or adjacent tasks.
