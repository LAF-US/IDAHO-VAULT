**Department Thirteen** is a top-secret arm of the government of [Ancelstierre](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Ancelstierre "Ancelstierre") responsible for gathering intelligence on neighbouring countries. Until the events of [The Creature in the Case](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/The_Creature_in_the_Case "The Creature in the Case"), Department Thirteen is run by [Alastor Dorrance](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Alastor_Dorrance "Alastor Dorrance").
