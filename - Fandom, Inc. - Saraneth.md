**Saraneth** is the sixth of the [Nine Bright Shiners](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Nine_Bright_Shiners "Nine Bright Shiners"). In [The Beginning](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/The_Beginning "The Beginning"), Saraneth became part of [the Charter](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/The_Charter "The Charter"). As a [bell](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Bells "Bells"), Saraneth is the Binder, which forces the listener to obey the wielder's will. It was wielded by [Sabriel](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Sabriel "Sabriel") at the binding of [Orannis](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Orannis "Orannis").

## Bell Sound

Saraneth is the deepest and lowest bell, with a very long, clear peal that cuts through the air like a foghorn.[^1]

## Trivia

- Saraneth is the favored bell of the Abhorsens and Sabriel's personal favorite bell.[^2]
- Saraneth is the most used and featured bell in the series, by both Abhorsens and [Free Magic](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Free_Magic "Free Magic") [necromancers](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Necromancer "Necromancer").
- Saraneth has the lowest note of any bell, despite [Astarael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Astarael "Astarael") being larger.
