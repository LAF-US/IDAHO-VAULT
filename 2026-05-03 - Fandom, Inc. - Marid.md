The Marid are the good-djinn tribe most directly associated with leadership, restraint, and ritualized obligation.

The page identifies them as rivals of the Ifrit and notes that they are comparatively few in number but considered the most formidable. Their customs include initiation rituals, and their tribal law requires young members to present reasonable wishes and justify them before the tribe council.

The Marid special animal is the camel. The page also notes that jade and copper are weaknesses that can be used in djinn seals against them.
