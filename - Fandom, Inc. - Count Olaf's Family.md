**Count Olaf's Family** is a mysterious part of 's past in *[A Series of Unfortunate Events](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/A_Series_of_Unfortunate_Events "A Series of Unfortunate Events").*

## Known Members

- [Count Olaf's father](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Olaf%27s_father "Count Olaf's father") †
- [Count Olaf's mother](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Olaf%27s_mother "Count Olaf's mother") †
- [Evelyn](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Evelyn "Evelyn") (Netflix series only- mentioned as an aunt of Olaf's) [^1]
- [Count Olaf](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Olaf "Count Olaf") †
- [Violet Baudelaire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Violet_Baudelaire "Violet Baudelaire") (formerly adoptive)
- [Klaus Baudelaire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Klaus_Baudelaire "Klaus Baudelaire") (formerly adoptive)
- [Sunny Baudelaire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Sunny_Baudelaire "Sunny Baudelaire") (formerly adoptive)

It is presumed that Count Olaf's title is legitimate, and thus either his father or mother (or both) are of noble birth. This is assumed due to the fact that Olaf seemed to be a student at [Wade Academy](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Wade_Academy "Wade Academy"), mainly attended by royal children.[^2]

[Count Olaf's mother](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Olaf%27s_mother "Count Olaf's mother") and [father](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Olaf%27s_father "Count Olaf's father") were killed at an opera performance of *[La Forza del Destino](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/La_Forza_del_Destino "La Forza del Destino")* by [Beatrice](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Beatrice_Baudelaire "Beatrice Baudelaire") and [Bertrand Baudelaire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Bertrand_Baudelaire "Bertrand Baudelaire"), with assistance from [Kit Snicket](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Kit_Snicket "Kit Snicket"),[^3] who was in a relationship with Olaf at some point in her life.

Olaf survived for some time afterward, until dying on [The Island](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/The_Island "The Island") from a harpoon gun wound.[^4]

### Speculation

As Kit Snicket's only confirmed relationship was with Olaf, it is often theorized that her daughter, [Beatrice Baudelaire II](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Beatrice_Baudelaire_II "Beatrice Baudelaire II"), is biologically Olaf's. While it is unknown when their breakup happened, the events of *[A Series of Unfortunate Events](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/A_Series_of_Unfortunate_Events "A Series of Unfortunate Events")* seem to take place under nine months, giving enough time for them to have had a physical relationship before he gained custody of [Violet](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Violet_Baudelaire "Violet Baudelaire"), [Klaus](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Klaus_Baudelaire "Klaus Baudelaire"), and [Sunny Baudelaire](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Sunny_Baudelaire "Sunny Baudelaire").

As Olaf's final words are quoting " [This Be The Verse](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/This_Be_The_Verse "This Be The Verse")," a poem about parents inevitably messing up their children, and he abused and tortured the Baudelaires in an effort to get revenge against their parents, it makes it much more thematic that the Baudelaire children adopt his biological child and plan to raise her with love and kindness and not continue the cycle of abuse.[^4]

In the Netflix series, Beatrice II is not Olaf's child and is known to be biologically the daughter of [Dewey Denouement](https://snicket.fandom.com/wiki/Dewey_Denouement "Dewey Denouement").[^5]
