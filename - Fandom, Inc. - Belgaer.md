**Belgaer** is the fifth of the [Nine Bright Shiners](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Nine_Bright_Shiners "Nine Bright Shiners"). In [The Beginning](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/The_Beginning "The Beginning"), Belgaer became part of [the Charter](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/The_Charter "The Charter"). As a [bell](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Bells "Bells"), Belgaer is the Thinker, who allows independent thought for the [dead](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death"), or erases memories. It was wielded by [Sameth](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Sameth "Sameth") at the binding of [Orannis](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Orannis "Orannis").

## Bell Sound

Belgaer is a loud bell, sounding one bright, clear note that can be felt in the bones of the head clear into the brain.[^1]

## Trivia

- Belgaer is only used twice in the series, save at the binding of Orannis: first by [Sabriel](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Sabriel "Sabriel") upon [Yrael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Yrael "Yrael") after their victory, to restore his entire self and grant him freedom; and second in [Death](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death") by [Lirael](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Lirael "Lirael") upon " [Clariel](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Clariel "Clariel")," the fragment of [Chlorr of the Mask](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Chlorr_of_the_Mask "Chlorr of the Mask") 's spirit.
- Belgaer is the bell most scorned by [necromancers](https://oldkingdomwiki.fandom.com/wiki/Necromancer "Necromancer").
