Wan is the first Avatar. He fuses permanently with Raava during Harmonic
Convergence, becomes the origin point of the Avatar Cycle, and receives the
elements in the order that later defines the cycle: fire, air, water, earth.

Wan is the origin vessel for the Avatar rulesystem: a living person who becomes
the first durable bridge between humans and spirits.

## Vault Use

Wan is the origin record for cycle-start, fusion, and element-order mechanics.
